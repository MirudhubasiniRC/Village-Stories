### Gap diagnosed (2026-07-18 look test)

| | Liked (Village Nest vibe) | Our test |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Roof | Thick **palm/coconut thatch** | Mangalore terracotta tiles |
| Walls | Warm **earthen ochre / mud plaster** | Bright cream lime “pretty cottage” |
| Yard | Homestead: **tulsi**, kolam, chickens, **cattle shed** beside house | Neat garden + picket gate, no cows |
| Feel | Humble working farm compound | Cute tiled villa-cottage |

Layout/kattil/small scale were OK. Materials + homestead density were wrong.

### Approved change (LOCKED)

Main Family House exterior defaults to:

- **Thatch roof** (dried palm / coconut leaf thatch), thick textured.
- **Earthen / mud-ochre plaster** walls — warm brown-cream, not bright villa white.
- Short shallow thinnai, kayiru kattil, matkas, firewood.
- Homestead yard: packed earth, kolam, **Tulsi madam**, chickens; thatch **cow /
  goat / chicken** shelters nearby; **jackfruit, banana, mango** near house.
- Long establishes: dense front greens, **narrow village green path**, **close
  irregular neighbours** (not street grid), **river + paddy fields** in wider
  frame, well under big tree toward rear.
- **NO compound / perimeter wall** — yard opens to the path.
- Interior vibe: warm earthen plaster, dense clay pots, hurricane lamp,
  clock/calendar — cozy vs cool rain outside when raining.

**Neighbour roofs may still be tiled.** Main Family House stays thatch.

Mangalore tile on the Main Family House is **not** the default from `ep_004`.
