# Art Style

## Purpose

This document defines the official illustration style of Village Stories.

It establishes the permanent artistic language for all characters, environments, props, animals, and backgrounds.

Every visual asset produced for the series must follow these standards.

# Universal Application

This anime style applies to **everything visible** in Village Stories.

It is not only for Father.

It is not only for people.

Every approved frame must render all visible elements in the same illustration
language:

| Category | Style requirement |
| --- | --- |
| **All characters** | Same soft delicate linework, painterly digital finish, soft cel-style shading blended with brushwork |
| **All animals** | Same Ghibli-inspired digital anime finish, not photoreal fur |
| **All objects** | Same soft linework and painterly digital material rendering |
| **All places** | Richly illustrated digital backgrounds with atmospheric depth |
| **All plants** | Painterly botanical detail with atmospheric perspective |
| **All surfaces** | Simulated watercolor and gouache texture within digital anime finish |
| **Sky and weather** | Rain, clouds, sun, mist — same atmospheric anime treatment |

### Production rule

If a character looks anime but the wall behind them looks photoreal, reject the
image.

If an object looks detailed but uses a different rendering language than the
people, reject the image.

If a location looks like a stock photo background with anime characters pasted
on top, reject the image.

The entire frame must look like one animated series world — the same world shown
in `Father_Canon_Design_Reference.png`.

# Artistic Direction

Village Stories **people and Tamil world stay ours**. The **rendering** is
**2.5D polished digital anime** (CANON-2026-013) with cozy Desi-village Shorts
warmth — dense props, warm/cool light contrast — applied to early-1990s rural
Tamil Nadu settings.

The artwork must feel warm, grounded, emotionally readable, and culturally
specific — **our characters and places**, in polished digital finish,
not Indian folk-art illustration style.

The style combines:

* Clean 2D anime characters + soft volumetric 2.5D props/food/animals.
* Soft matte–gloss hybrid; soft, delicate linework with slight variation.
* Gentle, expressive designs with **Village Stories** proportions.
* **Mother:** slim oval face (not chubby); LARGE soft-almond eyes ~1/4 face
  height; long stylized anime lashes; soft pink blush; left-chin mole
  (CANON-2026-012/013). Other adults/kids: grounded ~1/5 until approved.
  Normal adult head — never bobblehead.
* Soft cel-style shading with smooth gradients; soft specular on props/food.
* Rich atmospheric backgrounds with optional soft bokeh; dense interior props.
* Warm lamp/stove key vs cooler outdoor lake/rain when both visible.
* Clear warm palette with gentle colour pop; soft diffused cinematic light —
  never muted whitewash, never neon.
* Natural South Indian features, clothing, and village geography as setting.

Do not drift toward hard flat cel blocks, Bollywood saturation, oversaturated
tropical Indian palettes, semi-realistic portraiture, Western cartoon, chibi
comedy, full Pixar CGI people, photorealism, or thick comic outlines.

# Illustration Style

Maintain throughout the series:

* Studio Ghibli-inspired digital anime aesthetic.
* Painterly digital rendering with simulated watercolor and gouache textures.
* Soft matte finish.
* Soft, delicate linework with slight variation.
* Soft cel-style shading blended with painted digital brushwork.
* Richly illustrated digital backgrounds with atmospheric depth.
* Clear warm palette with gentle colour pop and soft diffused light — never
  muted whitewash, never neon.

Avoid:

* Hard flat cel blocks without painted blending.
* 3D photorealistic rendering or plastic CGI.
* Photorealistic human rendering.
* Hyper-real faces.
* Chibi proportions.
* Western cartoon exaggeration.
* Comic-book thick outlines.
* Bollywood saturation or bright tropical Indian folk-art palettes.
* Generic low-detail modern TV anime.
* Oversaturated HDR colour.
* AI glossy textures.

# Character Design Language

Characters should appear:

* Warm and approachable.
* Naturally expressive.
* Clearly anime while remaining believable and dignified.
* Distinctly South Indian in facial features, skin tone, hair, and expression.
* Consistent throughout the series.
* Naturally attractive and warm, not stylized film-hero glamour.

Maintain:

* Natural anime facial anatomy and age-appropriate proportions.
* Warm wheatish to medium-brown South Indian skin tones.
* Expressive deep brown eyes with thick natural eyebrows.
* Natural nose, mouth, cheek, and jaw proportions.
* Soft, emotionally readable smiles.
* Grounded body language.
* Traditional clothing.

Expressions should communicate emotion through subtle anime facial changes
rather than exaggerated theatre.

Father's reference sheet is the baseline for **all** illustration quality in the
series — characters, animals, objects, places, and backgrounds must match the
same anime language.

# Facial Proportions

Maintain consistent modern anime proportions across all recurring characters.

### Mother (slim oval + anime beauty face — CANON-2026-012 / 013)

* Slim soft **oval** face, gently tapered chin — elegant adult proportions.
* **NOT** chubby cheeks, round baby face, or puffy face.
* **LARGE beautiful soft-almond** warm-brown eyes — approximately **1/4** of face
  height; clear bright catchlights / gentle sparkle.
* **Long thick stylized anime eyelashes** (upper-lash emphasis).
* Soft arched thin black brows framing the larger eye line.
* **Clear soft pink cheek blush** (pretty and visible, not heavy makeup).
* Small delicate nose with traditional South Indian warmth; soft face highlights OK.
* Soft natural pink lips with gentle smile.
* Precise red circular tilak bindi centered on forehead — mandatory every day.
* Small gold nose pin on left nostril.
* **Permanent small black mole on LEFT chin — never omit, never move.**
* **Normal adult head–body ratio** — never huge chibi / bobblehead.

Lock Mother to `Mother_Doorway_Beauty_Canon_Reference.png` (CANON-2026-014)
whenever the tool supports CHARACTER_IDENTITY.

### Father (naturalistic Ghibli-inspired anime face)

**Father does NOT use Mother's soft-round face shape.** Use a **strong structured
masculine face** with **prominent angular jawline** and slightly rectangular lean.

* Strong structured masculine face; prominent angular jawline; masculine cheekbone structure.
* Expressive but grounded warm brown eyes — approximately 1/5 of face height.
* Steady calm gaze with subtle catchlights.
* Naturally thick straight-to-gently-angled eyebrows.
* Sharp nose — straight clean bridge, defined tip.
* Medium fullness lips, soft closed-mouth smile when at ease.
* **Neatly trimmed solid black moustache**; clean-shaven jaw and chin.
* Medium-to-fair complexion with warm golden sun-kissed undertones (#E8C89A–#DDBF8A).

Lock to `Father_Canon_Design_Reference.png` when generating or reviewing Father.

Avoid for Father:

* Soft round face, weak jaw, narrow chin, boyish or feminine facial proportions.
* Clean-shaven or heavy-beard redesigns.

**General character avoid (all people):**

* Chibi proportions.
* Hyper-realistic anatomy.
* Cartoon slapstick exaggeration.
* Extreme chibi black-dot eyes with no iris (Mother’s large eyes still show iris).
* Chubby / round baby / puffy Mother face.
* Huge bobblehead / chibi head proportions.
* Missing bindi on Mother.
* Missing or relocated Mother left-chin mole.

Characters should remain recognisable in every episode.

# Linework

Use:

* Soft, delicate linework with slight variation.
* Expressive contours on face, hair, clothing folds, and hands.
* Line weight variation only where it supports warmth and readability.
* Painted digital brushwork and shading working together with line art.

Avoid:

* Thick comic outlines.
* Missing line art with photoreal shading only.
* Harsh mechanical uniform strokes.
* Scratchy unstable contour drift between shots.

---

# Painterly Digital Finish And Shading

Surfaces should display:

* Soft cel-style shading blended with painted digital brushwork.
* Simulated watercolor and gouache textures within a digital anime finish.
* Soft matte finish on skin, cloth, wood, clay, and metal.
* Warm edge highlights from natural light.
* Gentle form gradients — not hard flat cel blocks without blending.
* Natural material readability within a painterly digital anime finish.
* Atmospheric depth in richly illustrated backgrounds.

Do not create:

* Hard flat cel blocks without painted blending.
* Plastic CGI material or 3D photorealistic rendering.
* Glossy AI skin or photoreal pore detail.
* Lineless airbrushed wallpaper art without delicate linework.

---

# Cinematic Lighting And Colour

Use:

* Soft diffused daylight or gentle cinematic light appropriate to time and
  weather.
* **Muted desaturated palette** — slate grey, moss green, dusty blue, earthy
  browns, warm tan skin tones, pale cream highlights.
* Japanese-influenced anime color grading on Indian village settings.
* Dreamy atmospheric depth without artificial bloom or fantasy haze.
* Controlled contrast that keeps faces and story action readable.

Avoid:

* Bollywood golden-orange saturation.
* Bright tropical oversaturated greens and yellows.
* Indian folk-art jewel-tone palettes.
* Neon colour treatment.
* Excessive bloom.
* High-contrast action-film lighting.
* Oversaturated HDR treatment.

Lighting direction, weather, and time of day remain governed by
`Lighting_Style.md`.

---

# Environmental Artwork

Backgrounds should receive the same anime artistic attention as characters.

Maintain:

* Rich environmental detail.
* Botanical accuracy.
* Layered vegetation.
* Atmospheric perspective.
* Soft distance haze.
* Soft-gradient anime skies with atmospheric depth.
* Natural terrain variation.

Every environment should feel like a place someone could explore.

---

# Botanical Detail

Plants should never appear as generic green shapes.

Render with:

* Individually defined leaves.
* Natural leaf variation.
* Layered foliage.
* Visible fruit where appropriate.
* Realistic growth patterns.
* Seasonal variation.

Special attention should be given to:

* Coconut trees.
* Banana plants.
* Mango trees.
* Paddy fields.
* Lotus plants.
* Village gardens.

---

# Animals

Animals should follow the same anime artistic language as the people.

Maintain:

* Soft expressive anime eyes.
* Natural proportions.
* Soft-gradient painterly fur and feathers with anime finish.
* Gentle personality.
* Believable anatomy.

Animals should feel like members of the village rather than decorative props.

---

# Props

Traditional objects should be illustrated with care.

Examples include:

* Clay pots.
* Brass vessels.
* Wooden furniture.
* Grinding stones.
* Bamboo baskets.
* Farming tools.
* Oil lamps.
* Traditional kitchen utensils.

Objects should show craftsmanship, wear, and everyday use.

---

# Background Detail

Backgrounds should support immersion without competing for attention.

Foreground:

Highest detail.

Midground:

Primary story area.

Background:

Atmospheric simplification with preserved identity.

The audience should immediately understand the location while remaining focused
on the story.

---

# Consistency

Every episode should feel like it belongs to the same animated series.

Do not change:

* Anime line quality.
* Painterly digital shading language.
* Character proportions.
* Colour palette family.
* Illustration finish.
* Emotional tone of the artwork.

Visual evolution should occur only through intentional improvements applied
consistently across the series and anchored back to the Father canon reference
sheet.
