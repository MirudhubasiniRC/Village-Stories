# Series Style Lock

## Purpose

The Series Style Lock is the permanent creative standard for the Village Stories universe.

It defines the artistic, emotional, visual, audio, and storytelling identity of the series.

Every production task—whether planning a story, generating images, creating animation prompts, designing sound, or reviewing quality—must follow the standards defined within this folder.

The Style Lock exists to ensure that every episode feels like it belongs to the same living universe, regardless of when it is produced or which AI or creator contributes to it.

# Folder Contents

## Project_Identity.md

Defines the purpose, emotional goals, audience experience, and creative mission of Village Stories.

---

## Visual_Identity.md

Defines the overall visual language that every frame should follow.

---

## Storytelling_Identity.md

Defines pacing, emotional philosophy, narrative style, and storytelling principles.

---

## Art_Style.md

Defines rendering style, illustration techniques, character rendering, and environmental artwork.

---

## Lighting_Style.md

Defines lighting families, light behaviour, atmosphere, and shadow consistency.

---

## Color_Language.md

Defines the official colour palettes and colour philosophy of the series.

---

## Materials_And_Textures.md

Defines the appearance and behaviour of traditional materials including wood, clay, stone, fabric, metal, vegetation, and weathering.

---

## Camera_Language.md

Defines shot selection, camera movement, framing, composition, and cinematic rhythm.

---

## Animation_Principles.md

---

Defines ambient sound, ASMR philosophy, environmental audio, silence, and sound design.

Defines historical authenticity, cultural accuracy, traditional lifestyle, architecture, clothing, tools, occupations, and everyday village life.

---

## Production_Quality.md

Defines the minimum production standard required before any work is considered complete.

---

# General Rules

* Every document inside this folder is authoritative.
* Do not duplicate information between documents.
* If information belongs in another document, reference it rather than repeating it.
* When multiple documents apply, load only those relevant to the current task.
* All creative decisions should preserve the established identity of Village Stories.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Series Style Lock protects the identity of Village Stories.

Stories may change.

Characters may grow.

The village may evolve.

But the creative identity of the series should remain instantly recognizable across every episode.
