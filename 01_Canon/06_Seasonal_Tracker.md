# 06_Season_Tracker.md

# Purpose

The Season Tracker is the official seasonal record of the Village Stories universe.

It records **which season** every story belongs to and ensures that the village evolves naturally over time.

This document does **not** describe how seasons behave. Environmental characteristics, farming, weather, plants, lighting, and seasonal activities are maintained in the World documentation.

---

# Season Philosophy

The village follows one continuous natural cycle.

Every episode belongs to a specific season.

The audience should experience the passage of time through changes in nature, farming, weather, festivals, and daily life.

The season should never change unexpectedly within an episode unless the story intentionally includes a time jump.

# Seasonal Continuity Rules

Before beginning production, verify that:

* The selected season matches the planned story.
* Farming activities match the season.
* River, pond, and canal conditions match the season.
* Vegetation matches the season.
* Animal behaviour matches the season.
* Clothing is appropriate for the weather.
* Festivals occur in the correct season.
* Lighting and atmosphere are consistent.

---

# Season Transitions

Season changes should occur only when the Universe Timeline indicates sufficient time has passed.

A seasonal transition should be noticeable through natural environmental changes rather than explicit explanation.

Examples include:

* River level rising after monsoon.
* Paddy fields progressing from planting to harvest.
* Trees flowering or fruiting.
* Different birds becoming more common.
* Festival decorations appearing.

---

# Exceptional Weather

Temporary weather events do not change the current season.

Examples include:

* One rainy day during summer.
* Several cloudy days.
* Strong winds.
* Morning mist.

These should be documented in the episode if they significantly affect the story but do not require changing the official season.

---

# Season Verification Checklist

Before approving an episode:

✓ Episode assigned to one season.

✓ Timeline agrees with the season.

✓ Farming activities are correct.

✓ Environmental conditions are correct.

✓ Festivals match the season.

✓ Story recorded in the Seasonal Register.

---

# Guiding Principle

The audience should never need to be told what season it is.

The village itself should naturally reveal the answer through its people, landscape, weather, and daily life.
