# Household Habits

## Purpose

This document supports natural domestic continuity.

## Repeating Habits

* Sweep, then prepare the courtyard for the day.
* Rinse, dry, and return vessels to their defined storage places.
* Fold and reuse cloths; mend practical items before replacing them.
* Set aside food scraps or feed only in safe, established animal-care contexts.
* Remove footwear or settle respectfully before indoor or devotional moments when appropriate.
* Offer water and a seat before extended conversation with a visitor.

## Rule

Habits are quiet evidence of care. Do not over-explain them in dialogue or repeat every habit in every scene.
