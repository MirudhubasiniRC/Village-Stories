# Forgotten Occupations

## Purpose

This reference preserves practical village work that may appear briefly around the established main cast.

## Useful Background Roles

* Washerman collecting, washing, drying, and returning clothes where story context establishes the service.
* Flower seller or garland maker carrying fresh flowers in a basket.
* Palm-leaf weaver repairing mats, baskets, or small roof materials.
* Itinerant umbrella, utensil, or tool repairer arriving by foot or bicycle.
* Seasonal agricultural helpers joining planting or harvest work.
* Postman delivering letters, money orders, and occasional postcards along a regular village route, usually by bicycle or on foot.

## Use Rule

These people are functional background life unless a story deliberately promotes them to named-character canon.

Do not create a fixed appearance, home, or relationship for a recurring individual without documenting that decision in `03_Characters/`.
