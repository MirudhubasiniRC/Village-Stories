# Forgotten Technology

## Purpose

This document keeps technology appropriate to the village's early-1990s setting.

## Appropriate Technology

* Transistor radio for occasional news, music, or cricket commentary.
* Simple household electric bulbs or tube lights where existing location canon permits electricity.
* Kerosene lanterns and oil lamps when electric light is unavailable or unsuitable.
* Hand tools, hand grinding, coir rope, bicycle transport, and bullock-cart work.
* Basic weighing scales at the grocery store.

## Technology Limits

Technology must remain modest, shared, and practical. It cannot make the village operate with contemporary speed, convenience, or visual polish.

## Avoid

Smartphones, LED strips, television-centred scenes, modern appliances, digital payment, online delivery, and contemporary agricultural machinery.
