# Animals

## Purpose

This is a research reference for natural village animal behaviour.

## Everyday Animal Life

* Cows and calves move slowly between shelter, water, and feed.
* Goats investigate edges of courtyards and paths under sensible supervision.
* Chickens scratch, gather, and return toward shelter near evening.
* Birds mark morning, heat, rain, and dusk through varied calls.
* The Family Dog has a specific German Shepherd character canon and must not be replaced by generic dog behaviour.

## Rule

Animals have needs, routine, and agency; they are not decorative props or comic chaos.

## Authority

Species and family-animal details remain governed by `02_World/Nature/Fauna.md` and the relevant character documentation.
