# Smells

## Purpose

This reference informs scene atmosphere and should never be treated as visual clutter.

## Recurring Sensory Notes

* Wet red earth after rain.
* Woodsmoke and early cooking.
* Freshly ground spices, coffee or tea, and hot rice.
* Hay, cattle shelter, sun-warmed wood, and clean drying cloth.
* Jasmine, temple flowers, turmeric, incense, and lamp oil in appropriate devotional contexts.

## Rule

Use scent as an emotional cue connected to an action or location. Keep all environments clean, cared for, and dignified.
