# Sounds

## Purpose

This is the cross-location sound reference for the village.

## Daytime

* Birds, chicken calls, goats, cattle movement, and distant human conversation.
* Hoe into soil, sickle through grass, water movement, basket rustle, and cart creak.
* Bicycle bell, footsteps on packed earth, and shop or tea-stall activity.

## Evening

* Insects, quieter birds, household vessels, low conversation, and distant radio where appropriate.
* Lamps being prepared, cattle settled, and doors or wooden fittings moving softly.

## Festival

* Temple bell, folk music, modest loudspeaker announcements, crowd talk, and temporary light-generator context only where documented.

## Rule

Natural sound is primary. Music supports emotion; it must not erase the village's ordinary sonic identity.
