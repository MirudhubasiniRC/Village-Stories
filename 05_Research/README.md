# Research

## Purpose

This folder gathers historical and sensory references that strengthen the early-1990s rural Tamil Nadu setting.

Research supports canon but does not override it. When a research note conflicts with an established canon document, the canon document remains authoritative until deliberately revised.

---

# Scope

Use this folder for:

* Forgotten daily-life details.
* Period-appropriate materials and technology.
* Work, trade, and household practices.
* Sounds, smells, weather, and seasonal observation.
* Historical mistakes to avoid.

---

# Research Documents

* `Forgotten_Village_Life.md` — everyday details that make the village feel lived in.
* `Forgotten_Occupations.md` — supporting trades and seasonal workers.
* `Forgotten_Communication.md` — pre-mobile-phone village communication.
* `Forgotten_Technology.md` — period-appropriate tools and technology limits.
* `Village_Economy.md` — small-scale buying, repair, exchange, and seasonal income.
* `Sounds.md` — natural, household, work, and festival sound language.
* `Smells.md` — sensory atmosphere connected to real action and place.
* `Plants.md` — grounded planting and seasonal greenery.
* `Animals.md` — natural animal-life rules.
* `Household_Habits.md` — quiet repeating domestic practices.
* `Common_Historical_Mistakes.md` — final anachronism and stereotype check.

---

# Research Rule

Do not turn a researched possibility into permanent canon automatically. Confirm it against the existing world, character, location, and object documentation before using it as a recurring production detail.

---

# Guiding Principle

Research should make the familiar feel specific: ordinary life remembered accurately.
