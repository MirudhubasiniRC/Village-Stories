# Plants

## Purpose

This document gives a stable natural language for village planting and greenery.

## Established Visual Families

* Paddy around cultivated fields.
* Coconut and banana near homes or wet ground where established.
* Neem, banyan, and shade trees at public gathering points.
* Tulsi and practical flowering plants near courtyards.
* Jasmine and other flowers for garlands and devotion where naturally grown or purchased.

## Rule

Plant placement follows water, shade, season, and location. Do not use ornamental landscaping, exotic garden design, or unrealistically lush greenery in every season.

## Authority

Specific location planting remains governed by the corresponding files in `02_World/`.
