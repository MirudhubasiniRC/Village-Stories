# Common Historical Mistakes

## Purpose

Use this checklist before creating a scene set in Village Stories.

## Do Not Introduce

* Smartphones, social media, digital payment, or modern delivery culture.
* LED lighting, contemporary decorative lighting, or modern appliance-heavy interiors.
* Modern cars, motorcycles, tractors, and sports bicycles as routine village transport.
* Branded packaging, logos, modern fashion, or plastic-dominant set dressing.
* Supermarket behaviour, café culture, or modern school technology.

## Do Not Misrepresent

* Rural life as frozen in the past, uniformly poor, or visually dirty.
* Tradition as an excuse for caricature.
* Animals as background scenery without care, routine, or safety.
* Women, elders, workers, or tradespeople as one-note stereotypes.

## Final Check

Ask: could this object, behaviour, sound, or convenience reasonably belong in this specific early-1990s village on this day?
