# Time of Day

## Purpose

This document defines the permanent characteristics of each time period within the Village Stories universe.

Every story, image prompt, animation, and environmental description should follow these definitions to ensure consistent lighting, atmosphere, village activity, wildlife behaviour, and family routines.

Time should influence the entire world, not just the lighting.

---

# Philosophy

Time changes everything.

As the sun moves across the sky, the village changes naturally.

Light changes.

Sounds change.

People change their activities.

Animals behave differently.

Cooking changes.

The atmosphere evolves continuously.

Every time period should immediately feel recognizable.

---

# Daily Timeline

The village permanently follows this daily sequence.

```text
Pre-Dawn

↓

Sunrise

↓

Early Morning

↓

Morning

↓

Midday

↓

Afternoon

↓

Golden Hour

↓

Evening

↓

Night

↓

Late Night
```

This progression remains constant throughout the series.

# Pre-Dawn

Approximate Time

4:30 AM – 5:30 AM

## Lighting

* Very low light.
* Deep blue sky.
* Soft silhouettes.
* Stars visible.
* Occasional moonlight.

## Village Activity

* Mostly quiet.
* A few early farmers waking.
* Kitchen preparations beginning.
* Oil lamps visible.

## Nature

* Crickets.
* Frogs during monsoon.
* Gentle breeze.

## Atmosphere

Peaceful.

Still.

Anticipation of sunrise.

---

# Sunrise

Approximate Time

5:45 AM – 6:30 AM

## Lighting

* Warm golden light.
* Long shadows.
* Mist when appropriate.
* Fresh colours.

## Village Activity

* Sweeping front yards.
* Kolam being drawn.
* Morning prayers.
* Fetching water.
* Tea preparation.

## Nature

* Birds become active.
* Butterflies begin appearing.
* Dew on plants.

## Atmosphere

Fresh.

Hopeful.

Quietly energetic.

---

## Village Activity

# Midday

Approximate Time

11:00 AM – 2:00 PM

## Lighting

* Strong overhead sunlight.
* Short shadows.

## Village Activity

* Lunch preparation.
* Farming slows.
* Some villagers rest indoors.

## Nature

* Birds quieter.
* Animals seek shade.

## Atmosphere

Warm.

Quiet.

Unhurried.

# Golden Hour

Approximate Time

4:30 PM – Sunset

## Lighting

* Rich golden light.
* Long shadows.
* Warm colours.

## Village Activity

* Livestock returning.
* Tea preparation.
* Children playing.
* Neighbours chatting.

## Nature

* Birds returning.
* Soft breeze.

## Atmosphere

---

## Village Activity

* Family conversations.

## Nature

Peaceful.

Family-oriented.

Comforting.

---

## Village Activity

* Quiet conversations.

## Nature

* Crickets.

## Atmosphere

Calm.

Restful.

# Late Night

Approximate Time

10:30 PM – 4:30 AM

## Lighting

Peaceful.

# Seasonal Influence

Every time period changes slightly with the seasons.

Examples:

Summer

* Earlier sunrise.
* Brighter afternoons.

Monsoon

* Diffused daylight.
* Darker afternoons.
* Earlier cloud cover.

Post-Monsoon

* Crisp mornings.
* Pleasant evenings.

Seasonal behaviour should remain consistent with the World documentation.
