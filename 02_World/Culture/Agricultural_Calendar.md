# Agricultural Calendar

## Purpose

This document defines the annual agricultural cycle followed within the Village Stories universe.

It establishes how farming, crop growth, harvesting, irrigation, livestock care, vegetable gardening, and seasonal food production change throughout the year.

Every story, image prompt, animation, and production document involving farming or food should remain consistent with this calendar.

---

# Philosophy

Agriculture is the foundation of village life.

The rhythm of farming influences:

* Family routines.
* Village activity.
* Seasonal cooking.
* Market produce.
* Landscape appearance.
* Wildlife behaviour.
* Festivals.

The land quietly determines the pace of everyday life.

---

# Annual Farming Cycle

The village follows this repeating agricultural cycle.

```text id="e5m5e4"
Land Preparation

↓

Sowing

↓

Early Growth

↓

Monsoon Growth

↓

Crop Maturity

↓

Harvest

↓

Field Preparation

↓

Cycle Repeats
```

This cycle repeats every year with seasonal variations.

---

# Land Preparation

Typical Season

Late Cool Season → Early Summer

## Activities

* Ploughing fields.
* Repairing bunds.
* Cleaning irrigation channels.
* Preparing seedbeds.
* Repairing farm tools.

## Landscape

* Open fields.
* Freshly turned soil.
* Dry pathways.
* Farmers working early mornings.

---

# Sowing

Typical Season

Early Monsoon

## Activities

* Planting paddy.
* Sowing millet.
* Planting vegetables.
* Water management.
* Nursery preparation.

## Landscape

* Small green shoots.
* Wet fields.
* Active farmers.

The village begins to turn greener.

---

# Early Growth

Typical Season

Monsoon

## Activities

* Weed removal.
* Irrigation.
* Fertilizing.
* Protecting young crops.

## Landscape

* Bright green fields.
* Growing vegetables.
* Healthy gardens.
* Active wildlife.

---

# Monsoon Growth

Typical Season

Peak Monsoon

## Activities

* Daily crop inspection.
* Water regulation.
* Garden maintenance.
* Livestock care.

## Landscape

* Lush vegetation.
* Flowing canals.
* Full ponds.
* Deep green countryside.

This is the most vibrant period of the agricultural year.

---

# Crop Maturity

Typical Season

Post-Monsoon

## Activities

* Monitoring crops.
* Preparing storage.
* Repairing harvest tools.
* Planning harvest.

## Landscape

* Mature vegetables.
* Heavy grain heads.
* Productive gardens.
* Fruit trees bearing produce.

---

# Harvest

Typical Season

Harvest Season

## Activities

* Harvesting paddy.
* Collecting vegetables.
* Drying grain.
* Transporting crops.
* Community cooperation.

## Landscape

* Golden fields.
* Harvest bundles.
* Active villagers.
* Full grain storage.

This is one of the busiest and happiest periods of the year.

---

# Rest & Preparation

After harvest, fields gradually return to a resting state.

Typical activities:

* Clearing crop residue.
* Compost preparation.
* Tool maintenance.
* Livestock grazing.
* Planning the next season.

The landscape becomes quieter before the cycle begins again.

Monsoon

* Pumpkin

Post-Monsoon

* Chillies
* Beans

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Seasonal farming.
* Appropriate crops.
* Logical harvest timing.
* Traditional farming methods.
* Realistic landscape changes.

Never:

* Show mature crops immediately after planting.
* Mix incompatible seasonal produce.
* Ignore the agricultural calendar.
* Introduce modern mechanized farming unless required by the story.
