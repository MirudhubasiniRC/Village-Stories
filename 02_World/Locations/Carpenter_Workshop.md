# Carpenter Workshop

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the village Carpenter's open-sided woodworking workshop.

---

# Location

The workshop is permanently located west of the Village Centre in the trades area, near the Small Grocery Store, Barber Under the Big Tree, and Blacksmith Forge routes.

---

# Identity

Maintain:

* Open-sided tile-roof shed.
* Shaded wooden veranda work edge.
* Packed-earth floor.
* Low workbench.
* Hand saws, chisels, plane, mallet, measuring string, and wood offcuts.
* Stacked timber under cover.
* No electric workshop machinery or modern showroom.

---

# Daily Life

The Carpenter builds and repairs doors, stools, shelves, furniture, cart woodwork, and household fittings through traditional hand-tool work.

---

# Atmosphere and Sounds

* Hand saw through wood.
* Plane shavings.
* Chisel taps.
* Wooden mallet.
* Timber creak.
* Shade-tree birds and low path conversation.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain a west-side open, tile-roof, hand-tool workshop.

Never create a modern enclosed factory, power-tool workshop, or furniture showroom.

# Guiding Principle

The workshop should feel like a place where wood gains a second life through patience, measurement, and hand skill.
