# Flora

## Purpose

This document defines the permanent plant life of the Village Stories universe.

It establishes the trees, crops, flowers, herbs, shrubs, grasses, and aquatic plants that naturally exist throughout the village.

Every future story, image prompt, storyboard, and animation should use this document as the authoritative reference for vegetation.

---

# Philosophy

The village is surrounded by living greenery.

Plants are not decoration.

They provide:

* Food.
* Medicine.
* Shade.
* Building materials.
* Habitat for wildlife.
* Seasonal beauty.

The audience should feel that the village grows naturally with the land.

---

# Vegetation Principles

Always maintain:

* Native South Indian vegetation.
* Natural growth patterns.
* Seasonal variation.
* Healthy but imperfect appearance.
* Organic placement.

Avoid ornamental landscaping.

The environment should feel cultivated through generations rather than professionally designed.

# Seasonal Behaviour

Vegetation changes naturally.

Summer

* Dry grass.
* Healthy mature trees.

Monsoon

* Rapid new growth.
* Deep green foliage.

Post-Monsoon

* Peak greenery.
* Flowering plants.
* Abundant vegetables.

Harvest Season

* Mature crops.
* Golden paddy fields.

Cool Season

* Fresh mornings.
* Dew-covered plants.

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Native South Indian species.
* Seasonal growth.
* Healthy gardens.
* Logical plant placement.
* Traditional farming.

Never:

* Introduce exotic ornamental plants without narrative reason.
* Show inconsistent seasonal growth.
* Place crops where they would not naturally grow.
