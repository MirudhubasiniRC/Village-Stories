# Weather

## Purpose

This document defines the permanent weather conditions of the Village Stories universe.

Weather influences lighting, atmosphere, farming, cooking, wildlife, village activities, and emotional tone.

Every story, image prompt, animation, and production document should apply weather consistently with the current season and time of day.

---

# Philosophy

Weather is never simply background.

It changes how people work.

It changes how food is prepared.

It changes how animals behave.

It changes how light enters the home.

It changes how the audience feels.

Weather should quietly become another storyteller.

---

# Weather System

The Village Stories universe uses naturally occurring rural South Indian weather.

Typical weather includes:

* Clear
* Partly Cloudy
* Overcast
* Light Rain
* Steady Rain
* Heavy Rain
* Mist
* Windy
* Hot & Dry

Weather should transition naturally rather than changing abruptly.

---

# Clear Weather

## General Feeling

Bright.

Open.

Peaceful.

Ideal for everyday village life.

---

## Sky

Maintain:

* Deep blue sky.
* Small white clouds.
* Excellent visibility.

---

## Lighting

Maintain:

* Bright sunlight.
* Strong natural colours.
* Defined shadows.

---

## Village Activity

Typical activities:

* Farming.
* Washing clothes outdoors.
* Children playing.
* Drying grains.
* Outdoor cooking preparation.

---

## Nature

Increase:

* Butterfly activity.
* Birdsong.
* Bees.
* Dragonflies.

---

# Partly Cloudy

## General Feeling

Comfortable.

Relaxed.

Balanced.

---

## Lighting

Maintain:

* Soft sunlight.
* Gentle cloud shadows.
* Reduced contrast.

---

## Village Activity

Normal daily routines continue.

Outdoor work remains comfortable.

---

## Nature

Maintain active birds and insects.

Light breeze.

---

# Overcast

## General Feeling

Quiet.

Cool.

Reflective.

---

## Sky

Maintain:

* Grey cloud cover.
* Soft horizon.
* Diffused light.

---

## Lighting

Maintain:

* Soft shadows.
* Cool colour tones.
* Even illumination.

---

## Village Activity

Cooking scenes feel especially cozy.

---

# Light Rain

## General Feeling

Peaceful.

Comforting.

Refreshing.

---

## Rain

Maintain:

* Gentle rainfall.
* Water droplets on leaves.
* Soft ripples in puddles.

---

## Lighting

Maintain:

* Diffused daylight.
* Soft reflections.
* Reduced contrast.

---

## Nature

Increase:

* Frog calls.
* Wet foliage.
* Fresh earth.

---

## Kitchen Influence

Through the Garden Window viewers may see:

* Gentle rain.
* Moving leaves.
* Steam contrasting with cool air.

This weather is ideal for ASMR cooking scenes.

---

# Steady Rain

## General Feeling

Warm indoors.

Cool outdoors.

Relaxing.

---

## Rain

Maintain:

* Continuous rainfall.
* Wet pathways.
* Overflowing roof edges.
* Water flowing toward fields.

---

## Village Activity

Outdoor work becomes limited.

Indoor cooking increases.

Families remain closer together.

---

## Nature

Maintain:

* Active frogs.
* Flowing streams.
* Fresh vegetation.

---

# Heavy Rain

## General Feeling

Powerful.

Dramatic.

Safe indoors.

---

## Rain

Maintain:

* Dense rainfall.
* Splashing water.
* Strong roof runoff.
* Fast-moving water in canals.

---

## Village Activity

Most activities move indoors.

Travel becomes limited.

Cooking often becomes the centre of family life.

---

## Kitchen Influence

The warm Kitchen contrasts beautifully with the storm outside.

Steam, firelight, and rainfall should work together to create a feeling of comfort.

---

# Mist

## General Feeling

Quiet.

Fresh.

Dreamlike.

---

## Typical Time

Most common:

* Early morning.
* After rainfall.

---

## Lighting

Maintain:

* Soft diffusion.
* Reduced visibility.
* Gentle colours.

---

## Nature

Dew on leaves.

Spider webs.

Fresh vegetation.

---

# Windy Weather

## General Feeling

Lively.

Refreshing.

---

## Environmental Behaviour

Maintain:

* Banana leaves swaying.
* Coconut trees moving.
* Hanging cloths fluttering.
* Smoke drifting naturally.

---

## Kitchen Influence

Gentle airflow enters through:

* Garden Window.
* Courtyard Door.
* Rear Veranda Door.

The Kitchen should feel naturally ventilated.

---

# Hot & Dry

Most common during Peak Summer.

Maintain:

* Bright sunlight.
* Dry soil.
* Heat shimmer.
* Minimal cloud cover.

Village activity shifts toward morning and evening.

* Rainwater dripping from roof tiles.

* Dust during summer.
* Steam more visible in cool weather.
* Mist over fields.
* Reflections in puddles.

These details strengthen realism.

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Seasonal consistency.
* Logical lighting.
* Appropriate wildlife behaviour.
* Realistic transitions.
* Natural environmental effects.

Never:

* Change weather abruptly.
* Contradict the active season.
* Ignore weather's influence on village life.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the weather system of the Village Stories universe.

All future stories, prompts, animations, and environmental descriptions should follow these weather rules.

---

# Guiding Principle

Weather should never merely decorate a scene.

It should quietly shape how the village looks, sounds, moves, cooks, and lives, allowing the audience to feel the changing world without needing it explained.
