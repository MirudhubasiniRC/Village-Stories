# Bullock Cart

The family vehicle is a traditional wooden bullock cart, not a tractor cart.

Maintain a weathered wooden bed and frame, two large wooden wheels with period-appropriate metal rims, wooden yoke, coir rope, and gentle practical repairs. It is stored beside the Hay Storage under the Livestock Shelter lean-to.

It carries hay, harvest baskets, and farming tools on short village journeys. Its sound is a slow wooden creak, wheel movement, and controlled animal footfall.

Bullocks are always handled calmly, rested appropriately, and never used as spectacle.
