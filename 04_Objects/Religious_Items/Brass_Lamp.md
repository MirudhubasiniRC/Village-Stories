# Brass Lamp

The recurring brass lamp is a traditional kuthuvilakku used in the Family Pooja Corner, Vinayagar Shrine, and suitable festival contexts.

Maintain a warm aged-brass body, stable traditional proportions, soft oil flame, cotton wick, and gentle careful polishing. It is devotional, never luxury décor.

An adult lights, carries, and extinguishes it safely. The lamp must not be moved between locations without a clear ritual or story reason.

Never render it as an electric decorative lamp, plastic item, or elaborate temple spectacle.
