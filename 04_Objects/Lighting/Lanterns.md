# Lanterns

## Purpose

This document defines recurring non-electric and festival lighting objects.

---

# Kerosene Lantern

Maintain a modest glass-and-metal kerosene lantern with a warm, low flame and naturally aged metal. It is suitable for dusk, power-unavailable contexts, and practical outdoor household use.

# Latinam Lanterns

Latinam lanterns (லத்தீன் விளக்கு) are temporary Festival Ground decorations. They hang on bamboo entrance frames, paths, and stall fronts, giving a warm swaying glow at dusk and night.

# Color Lights

Color-light strings are temporary early-1990s festival bulb strings, used with Latinam lanterns only during festival setup. They are not permanent village decoration.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never show LED strips, modern emergency lamps, or permanently installed fair lighting. Lighting must match the time of day, electricity context, and location canon.
