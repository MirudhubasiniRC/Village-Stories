# Episode Types

* Cooking and shared meals
* Family memories and relationships
* Rain and changing weather
* School and childhood
* Temple and festival preparation
* Animals and animal care
* Gardening, farming, and harvest
* Tea Stall, Grocery Store, and village errands
* Village professions and repair
* Neighbourhood and community help
* Seasonal and special-event stories

## Multi-Part Types

Use multi-part storytelling only when a real process continues:

* A crop, garden, repair, recipe, or festival preparation across days.
* A postman letter whose meaning unfolds through family conversation.
* A sibling disagreement followed by reflection and repair.
* A gentle Father–Mother reminiscence that connects past care to present family life.

Each part requires its own small resolution.
