# Endings

## Satisfying End Images

* Family sharing food.
* A repaired object returning to use.
* Children making peace through action.
* Father and Mother sharing a quiet recognition.
* Family Dog sleeping after the household settles.
* Rain easing, lamp light, prayer, sunset, or a wide village pull-away.

## Continuation Rule

If an episode continues, resolve today's feeling before planting tomorrow's question. For example, siblings apologise today; tomorrow they complete the task they delayed.

Do not end with unexplained sadness, danger, or a sudden cut made only for retention.
