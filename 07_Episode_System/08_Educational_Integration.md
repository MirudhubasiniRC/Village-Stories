# Educational Integration

Education stays inside the story.

* Cooking demonstrates care, measurement, hygiene, and sharing.
* Farming demonstrates seasons, patience, soil, and water.
* School demonstrates effort, listening, and confidence.
* Animal care demonstrates observation and responsibility.
* A family disagreement demonstrates listening, apology, and repair.

## Rule

Let viewers learn from choices and process. Never pause a story for a lecture, quiz, or forced moral.
