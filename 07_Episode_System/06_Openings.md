# Openings

Use an opening that immediately belongs to the episode:

* Sunrise, birds, rooster call, or first cooking steam.
* Rain beginning on tile or courtyard earth.
* A door opening, bicycle arriving, or Family Dog noticing someone.
* A garden harvest, school bag, kitchen fire, or work tool in use.
* A close-up of an object that carries a family memory.

## Rule

Open with the strongest truthful image, then orient the viewer quickly. Do not reuse the same sunrise opening by habit.
