# Episode Review System

## Before Release

Rate the episode for story clarity, emotion, visual continuity, audio, ASMR, educational integration, and rewatchability.

## After Release

Review:

* Audience retention and completion.
* Whether the opening accurately promised the episode.
* Comments that show emotional connection or confusion.
* Whether viewers followed an honest continuing thread.
* Rewatchability and thumbnail clarity.

## Rule

Use audience data to improve clarity and pacing. Do not chase short-term metrics by abandoning the series' calm, truthful story promise.
