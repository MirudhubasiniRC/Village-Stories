# Episode Philosophy

Every episode should feel like visiting the village: specific, calm, emotionally true, and complete.

* Everyday life is enough for a story.
* Each episode needs one memorable emotional or sensory moment.
* Viewers should leave calmer, warmer, more curious, or more hopeful.
* A continuing thread may invite the next episode, but the current episode must still resolve its own moment.

## Serialised Emotion

Longer threads are welcome when they grow naturally:

* Father and Mother remembering an early married-life moment through an object, recipe, song, or place.
* Elder Brother and Younger Daughter arguing, cooling down, understanding one another, and repairing the relationship over several Shorts.
* A family disappointment, lost hope, or non-graphic illness met with care, patience, and eventual comfort.

The series never turns these into melodrama, cruelty, or a romance-focused plot.
