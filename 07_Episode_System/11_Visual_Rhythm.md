# Visual Rhythm

## Alternate Framing

Move purposefully among:

* Wide shots for geography, weather, and village scale.
* Medium shots for relationships and practical work.
* Close-ups for emotion and reaction.
* Detail shots for hands, food, tools, water, fabric, animals, and nature.

## Rule

Mix character, nature, objects, and environment. Do not repeat the same framing or cut pattern without a story reason.
