# YouTube Shorts

## Structure

* Begin with a visible hook in the first seconds.
* Present one clear activity, feeling, or question.
* Keep captions clear of important visual action.
* Include one memorable thumbnail-worthy image.
* End with a completed emotional beat.

## Continuation Design

Use a connected Shorts sequence only for a naturally continuing event. Label parts clearly and make each part understandable for a new viewer.

Good retention comes from recognition, curiosity, and emotional trust—not from deceptive cliffhangers.
