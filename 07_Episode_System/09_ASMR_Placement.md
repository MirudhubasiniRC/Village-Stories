# ASMR Placement

Use close natural sound at moments of skilled, emotionally relevant action:

* Grinding, chopping, pouring, stirring, and steam.
* Rain, leaves, water, fire, birds, and footsteps.
* Bangles, vessels, coir rope, bicycle bell, and animal movement.

## Placement Rule

Use a short ASMR pause at the opening, during the main activity, or before the ending. Never layer every sound at once or let ASMR interrupt important emotional information.
