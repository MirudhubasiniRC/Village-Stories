# Episode Checklist

Before publishing, confirm:

* Canon, character behaviour, weather, objects, and locations are correct.
* The hook is truthful and appears immediately.
* The story has one clear emotional centre.
* Every scene moves the story or feeling forward.
* Audio, ASMR, music, and silence have a purpose.
* The ending is emotionally satisfying.
* A connected next episode has an honest seed, if used.
* Google Flow continuity and a thumbnail moment are present.
