# Scene Structure

```text
Opening image
↓
Need or emotional question
↓
Preparation
↓
Main activity
↓
Small obstacle or discovery
↓
Practical resolution
↓
Family or community moment
↓
Closing image / next-thread seed
```

## Rule

Every scene must progress the task, reveal a feeling, deepen place, or prepare a later payoff. Do not repeat the same visual information.
