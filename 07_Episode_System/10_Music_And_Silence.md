# Music and Silence

## Use Ambient Sound Alone

For concentrated work, rain, walking, cooking, animal care, and quiet observation.

## Use Soft Music

To support a gentle emotional turn, family memory, earned relief, or final warmth.

## Use Silence

After a meaningful look, during grief or reflection, before an apology, and in the final resting image.

## Rule

Music supports what the audience already understands. It must never tell viewers what to feel or cover the village soundscape.
