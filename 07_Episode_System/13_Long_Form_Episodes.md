# Long-Form Episodes

## Length

Long-form episodes may run five to ten minutes when the story has enough real process and emotional breathing room.

## Structure

Use a main thread with one light supporting thread, such as:

* A cooking task alongside a sibling repair.
* Festival preparation alongside Father and Mother's shared memory.
* Farm work alongside animal care.

## Rule

Longer runtime adds observation, process, and atmosphere—not filler, repeated conflict, or unrelated subplots.
