# Pacing Timeline

## YouTube Short: 60–120 Seconds

* 0–5 seconds: hook.
* 5–20 seconds: setting and need.
* 20–60 seconds: preparation and main action.
* 60–90 seconds: obstacle, discovery, or emotional turn.
* Final seconds: resolution, peaceful image, and optional next-thread seed.

## Rule

Shorts move quickly between story beats but slow down inside meaningful sensory actions. Do not fill every second with cuts, dialogue, or music.
