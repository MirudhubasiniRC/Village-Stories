## Scene 1 — Backyard / courtyard garden

**Time:** Early morning · Post-Monsoon/Harvest · Clear  
**Cast:** Elder Brother, Younger Daughter  
**Objects:** Wicker basket (brother, right hand); orange marigold in clay pot;
blue butterfly

---

### Shot 1 — Hook (4 s)

**Visual:** Extreme close on bright orange marigold in terracotta pot. Soft golden
morning light. A **blue butterfly** settles on the flower — wings folded, then
a slight twitch.

**Sound:** Garden birds distant; very soft breeze in leaves.

**Caption (optional):** none — image is the hook.

---

### Shot 2 — Orientation (6 s)

**Visual:** Medium shot — **Elder Brother** (checkered shirt, tan shorts,
wicker basket in right hand) and **Younger Daughter** (yellow floral dress, two
braids, small red bindi) stand on earth path beside cream wall and wooden gate.
Both look toward the marigold. Brother slightly taller — scale per
`Elder_Brother_Younger_Daughter_Scale_Reference.png`.

**Sound:** Footstep settle; basket faint creak; morning village stillness.

---

### Shot 3 — Process (8 s)

**Visual:** Medium, eye-level. Sister takes one small step closer, hands gently
together. Brother stays beside her, patient, basket lowered. Both faces soft
with curiosity — no shouting, no reaching to grab butterfly.

**Sound:** Soft fabric movement; distant hen cluck (single, far).

---

### Shot 4 — Turn insert (5 s)

**Visual:** Close on marigold and butterfly. Wings open slowly — blue iridescent
painterly anime detail. Morning side-light on petals.

**Sound:** Near-silent; one soft leaf rustle.

---

### Shot 5 — Gentle turn (7 s)

**Visual:** Medium two-shot. Butterfly lifts from marigold and drifts upward.
Children's eyes follow — sister's mouth opens in silent delight; brother's small
proud smile.

**Sound:** Barely audible wing flutter suggestion (natural, not cartoon buzz).

---

### Shot 6 — Resolution (6 s)

**Visual:** Medium. Butterfly exits frame upward. Brother picks a **fallen
marigold petal** from path, offers it to sister. She accepts with both small
hands, soft smile.

**Sound:** Soft earth scuff; gentle exhale feeling.

---

### Shot 7 — Release / rest (5 s)

**Visual:** Wide vertical courtyard — children small in warm garden, cream wall,
marigold pots, gate, soft peach morning sky. Hold peaceful; no new action.

**Sound:** Morning ambience swells slightly then settles; optional fade to near-
silence.

**End card caption (optional, 2 s overlay on hold):**

> Village Stories · early morning in the courtyard
