# Episode ep_001 — Premise

## Working title

**Butterfly Morning** (Tamil working title: *வண்ணாத்தி காலை*)

## One-sentence promise

In the early-morning family courtyard, Elder Brother and Younger Daughter pause
their small chores when a blue butterfly lands on a marigold — they watch it
together in quiet wonder, then return to the day with a gentle smile.

## Format

YouTube Short · **9:16** · **~55 seconds** · no cliffhanger · works muted

## Formula

**Animal-Care / Discovery** — everyday base (morning courtyard, brother with
basket); gentle turn (butterfly on marigold); human response (shared watching);
settled image (peaceful courtyard rest).

## Cast

| Character | Role |
| --- | --- |
| Elder Brother | Holds wicker basket from morning round; patient older sibling |
| Younger Daughter | Curious younger sister; reaches wonder first |

Mother, Father, Dog, and livestock **not in frame** — debut Short introduces
the children and courtyard world.

## Time and place

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Season | Post-Monsoon / Harvest |
| Weather | Clear, soft morning |
| Time | Early morning |
| Location | `02_World/Locations/Backyard.md` (family courtyard / garden edge) |

## Story spine

| Beat | Content |
| --- | --- |
| Practical need | Children moving through morning courtyard with small household help |
| Gentle obstacle | A beautiful distraction — butterfly on marigold — asks for attention |
| Practical response | Both stop, watch together without chasing or grabbing |
| Emotional turn | Shared quiet delight; butterfly lifts and flies |
| Ending rest | Brother offers a fallen marigold; courtyard holds in warm morning peace |

## What this episode is not

* Not a chase, catch, or “save the butterfly” plot
* Not a lesson lecture or narration-heavy explainer
* Not a multi-location montage
* Not a trailer for a longer episode
