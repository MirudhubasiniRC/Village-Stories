| Phase | When | What you receive |
| --- | --- | --- |

## Story flow — Clip 01 → 10 (revised)

| Clip | ID | ~Dur | Story beat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 01 | sh_001 | 4 s | **Hook** — Blue butterfly on marigold in terracotta pot. No people. |
| 02 | sh_002 | 6 s | **Orientation** — Siblings + German Shepherd discover butterfly at pot (same camera side as Clip 01, close to flower). Dog head tilt. |
| 03 | sh_003 | 5 s | **Turn + line 1** — Butterfly **starts to rise**. Younger Daughter says **`Butterfly…`** cutely, happy tone. **Elder Brother laughs** at his little sister’s sweet reaction. |
| 04 | sh_004 | 3 s | **Insert — calf** — Baby calf playfully nuzzles / head-butts hay. No horns, small brass bell. Same late-morning compound. |
| 05 | sh_005 | 3 s | **Insert — goats** — White goat + brown goat (droopy ears) drinking at stone trough. |
| 06 | sh_006 | 8 s | **Choppu saman — setup (macro)** — Children place choppu saman on **mini clay stove** surface. **Macro close-up**: pour **water** and add **sand** into toy bowl. |
| 07 | sh_007 | 8 s | **Choppu saman — mould (macro)** — **Macro close-up**: Elder Brother **moulds wet sand like idli** in his palm. |
| 08 | sh_008 | 6 s | **Choppu payoff (MS)** — **Continuity from idli sand** — pull to medium shot: full brother and sister **smiling** proudly at the sand idli. |
| 09 | sh_009 | 7 s | **Comedy + line 2** — **Continuity** — Family Dog **licks** smiling Younger Daughter’s face. She laughs: **`No…!`** |
| 10 | sh_010 | 8 s | **Release (wide)** — Camera **pulls back** to show **full courtyard place** — siblings, dog, choppu spot, garden; peaceful end. Fade to black in edit. |

**Emotional arc:** Wonder → discovery → sweet laugh → compound life → choppu play (macro → smile) → dog comedy → wide peaceful place.

**Removed from prior draft:** separate “pet dog” beat (Clip 03 old); dialogue moved from Clip 04 to Clip 03.

  watercolor/gouache, soft matte (`Art_Style.md`)
