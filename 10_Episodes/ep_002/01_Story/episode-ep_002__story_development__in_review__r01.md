## 1. Locked episode promise

> After a race the dog wins, Younger Daughter comes home in tears. Mother comforts her, cooks kesari once elachi is borrowed from Lakshmi Paati, and on the Front Thinnai the girl forgives the dog with a tiny palm-leaf share — afternoon settles with the daily calendar.

  Offer treat → kesari cutaway → agree → missing elachi → walk to Paati

- Cast: same + Dog watching

  - Mother: “Shall I make you something?”  
- Cutaway A: warm plated **kesari** (identity target for later cook)

### Scene 4 — Kitchen (NOW) · Gap
- Cast: Mother (Dog may wait at doorway — canon-safe)  
- Action: lay ghee tin, rava, sugar, cashews if used, water/milk vessel  
- Cutaway B: **elachi pods** (missing)  
- Light worry — not panic  

### Scene 5 — Path → N-04 (NOW) · Borrow
- Cast: Mother, Family Dog, Lakshmi Paati  
- Action: walk · elachi handoff CU · biscuit · palm-leaf eat  
- Keep Paati dignified, flower-garden house readable if establishing  

### Scene 6 — Kitchen (NOW) · Cook (long-form heart)
- Cast: Mother primarily; window birds; Dog optional at threshold  
- Stages (meaningful selection, not every atom):  
  1. Rava dry-roast scent gesture  
  2. Ghee warm  
  3. Elachi crushed/added  
  4. Liquid + sugar · stir  
  5. Soften · cashew if present  
  6. **Garden window glance — birds in tree** (atmosphere, not subplot)  
  7. Finish · rest off heat · spoon to cups  
- ASMR-led; almost no dialogue  

### Scene 7 — Front Thinnai (NOW) · Share & Forgive
- Cast: Full family sitting (Father, Mother, Elder Brother, Younger Daughter, Dog)  
- Afternoon dappled light per Thinnai lock  
- Beat: serve → dog hope → palm-leaf share → lick → laugh  
- Ending rest on thinnai together  

### Scene 8 — Calendar seal
- Same series calendar language as ep_001 · page fall · fade · next episode date available
