## 1. Episode Summary

Same day as *Butterfly Morning*, mid-afternoon (~3pm — warm light, **not dusk-dark**). Younger Daughter and the Family Dog race; the dog wins; she cries home. Mother comforts her (`it's okay, kanna`), offers to make something; the girl wants **kesari**. Mother lays out ingredients, realises **elachi (cardamom)** is missing, walks with the dog to **Lakshmi Paati** (N-04), who gives elachi and one biscuit to the dog. Mother cooks kesari (detailed process; birds at kitchen window). Family gathers on the **Front Thinnai**. Dog sits sadly beside the girl; she softens, shares a tiny amount on a palm leaf; dog licks her face; she laughs. Day ends with calendar page turn → fade (handoff to next episode date).

---

## 2. Emotional Goal

Forgiveness and soft humour after a child's wounded pride — Mother's warmth, sibling/shared sweet, and the girl choosing kindness to the dog without a lecture.

Primary feeling: **comfort → desire → small adventure of borrowing → shared joy → forgiveness**.

---

## 3. Educational Value (through action only)

* Borrowing one kitchen spice from a neighbour is normal village trust.
* Cooking kesari as visible process (ingredients, stir, patience).
* Care for animals: biscuit from Paati; small shared sweet on palm leaf — never wasteful or mockery.

No lecture-to-camera.
