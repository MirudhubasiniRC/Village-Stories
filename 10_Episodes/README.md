# Episodes Archive

## Purpose

`10_Episodes/` is the permanent home for episode-specific production records.

Each episode gets one stable folder. Canon, world, character, object, and
research truth stay in their authoritative folders. This archive holds what was
decided and produced for that episode.

---

# Folder Model

```text
10_Episodes/
├── README.md
└── ep_###/
    ├── README.md
    ├── 00_Admin/
    ├── 01_Story/
    ├── 02_References/
    ├── 03_Storyboard/
    ├── 04_Generation/
    ├── 05_Audio/
    ├── 06_Edit/
    ├── 07_QA/
    ├── 08_Release/
    └── 09_Archive/
```

Logical structure follows `08_Production/16_File_Organization.md`.

Naming follows `10_Studio_Standards/Naming_Standards.md` and
`10_Studio_Standards/Asset_Naming.md`.
