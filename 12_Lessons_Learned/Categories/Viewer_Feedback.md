## Rules

- No private personal data.
- Quote or paraphrase publicly useful signals only.
- Do not chase one toxic comment into canon change.
- Prefer patterns across multiple viewers / videos.

## Open patterns to watch

- Moments viewers rewatch or praise (ASMR, warmth, authenticity)
- Confusion about story/character identity
- Requests that would break tone or period — mark as `observe`, not auto-adopt
- Accessibility / caption feedback
