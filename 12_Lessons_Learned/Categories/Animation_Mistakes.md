## Open patterns to watch

- Multiple principal actions in one clip
- Speed-label language (“slowly”) causing tool drift
- Animals acting against species / Family Dog canon
- Environment static when micro-motion should live
- Timing mismatches vs board durations
- **Single-image used when previous approved frame is a required begin state**
- **Story result (win/lose/arrive) not readable in silhouette**
- **Camera always Static — Movement Bible unused**
