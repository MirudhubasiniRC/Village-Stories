# Neighbour Family 01 — Ramasamy Elder Couple

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical documentation for Neighbour Family 01: the elder couple living at House N-02, northwest of the Main Family.

The Village Neighbourhood map already identifies this home as the Ramasamy Family. That established house identity remains permanent; this folder gives its recurring residents functional production identities.

---

# Household Identity

Neighbour Family 01 is an elder couple who have lived in the village for many years.

They are trusted next-door elders rather than replacement grandparents for the main family. Their home is a familiar extension of the neighbourhood: a place of old stories, small gifts of garden produce, flowers for prayer, and patient observation.

They live close enough for natural doorstep visits, but each household retains privacy and independence.

---

# Residents

* Grandmother — approximately 65–70
* Grandfather — approximately 68–73

Their personal names are not required in regular production dialogue. Use their functional identities unless a future story earns a permanent name.

---

# Narrative Function

The elder couple represent:

* Living village memory.
* Traditional knowledge.
* Intergenerational affection.
* Calm neighbourly presence.
* Gentle storytelling.
* Everyday community care.

They enrich stories without taking focus away from the Main Family.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

They:

* Treat Elder Brother and Younger Daughter with grandparent-like affection.
* Share flowers, vegetables, and occasional homemade snacks.
* Exchange ordinary doorstep conversation with Mother.
* Offer Father quiet farming-memory or weather wisdom when appropriate.
* Know the Family Dog as a familiar, trusted visitor.

Their care is warm but never intrusive.

---

# Typical Story Use

Natural story settings include:

* Festival preparation.
* Flower sharing for the Pooja Corner.
* Herbal home-remedy knowledge.
* Memories of earlier village seasons.
* Younger Daughter listening to a story.
* Elder Brother learning respect for elders.
* Quiet front-thinnai observation.

# Household Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Elder-couple household identity.
* House N-02 / Ramasamy Family map continuity.
* Warm but independent neighbour relationship with the Main Family.
* Age-appropriate slower movement and practical wisdom.
* No unnecessary family drama.

Never:

* Make them a young couple.
* Move them to another house.
* Treat them as helpless by default.
* Force them into every episode.

---

# Canon Authority

This folder permanently defines Neighbour Family 01 within the Village Stories universe.

# Guiding Principle

Neighbour Family 01 should feel like the familiar elder home next door: a place where time moves a little more slowly and every ordinary visit carries warmth, memory, and quiet care.
