# Widow Aunty — Lakshmi Paati

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Widow Aunty of Neighbour Family 03 throughout the Village Stories universe.

She is Lakshmi Paati, the independent elder woman living at House N-04 southwest of the Main Family.

---

# Identity

Lakshmi Paati is approximately **65–72 years old**.

She is a widowed South Indian village elder known for her beautiful flower garden, jasmine cultivation, and handmade garlands for everyday worship and festival use.

Her life is peaceful, active, and dignified. Widowhood is a factual part of her life, not her entire personality or a source of continuous sadness.

---

# Narrative Role

She represents:

* Jasmine and flower-garden beauty.
* Quiet independence.
* Temple-linked care.
* Generational patience.
* Village softness outside the Main Family home.

---

# Appearance

Maintain:

* Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion with natural elder lines.
* Slim-to-medium healthy elder build.
* Dark brown eyes, gentle and observant.
* Silver-grey hair in a neat low bun or simple practical braid.
* Small muted bindi when appropriate to her established everyday tradition.
* Simple traditional earrings or studs.
* A calm, upright but age-appropriate posture.

Her face should communicate warmth and competence, never fragility or cosmetic youth.

---

# Clothing

Everyday:

* Simple cotton saree in gentle traditional shades: white with a coloured border, muted green, maroon, soft blue, cream, or mustard.
* Practical blouse.
* Minimal jewellery.
* Bare feet in garden and house when practical; simple sandals outdoors.

Garden work:

* Saree edge tucked lightly for ease.
* Small flower basket or cloth possible.

Festival:

* Clean handloom or fine cotton saree.
* Fresh jasmine only when she is not actively working with garlands.
* Modest traditional presentation.

Never use heavy makeup, modern fashion, or elaborate glamour jewellery.

---

# Personality

She is:

* Kind.
* Independent.
* Patient.
* Gentle.
* Quietly humorous.
* Practical.
* Spiritually grounded without preachiness.
* Protective of flowers and living things.

She does not pity herself and does not invite pity from others.

---

# Skills and Knowledge

She naturally knows:

* Jasmine cultivation.
* Flower garden care.
* Harvesting flowers at the right time.
* Garland-making.
* Preserving flowers in shade and water briefly.
* Simple temple offerings and decoration.
* Basic home-garden knowledge.
* Everyday herbal plant awareness.

She understands that serious illness or plant disease beyond simple care may require specialist help.

---

# Behaviour

She naturally:

* Tends jasmine and flowering plants in cooler morning and evening hours.
* Picks blooms carefully into a small basket.
* Sorts stems with patient fingers.
* Threads garlands on the thinnai or shaded work spot.
* Shares flowers with Mother for prayer when natural.
* Lets children watch and try a small safe section of garland work.
* Keeps her garden orderly without making it ornamental perfection.

She avoids waste and picks only what is ready.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

## Mother

Mother and Lakshmi Paati share flower, prayer, garden, and festival familiarity. Their relationship is respectful, warm, and practical.

## Father

Father treats her with respectful neighbourly care. He may help with a heavy repair or weather protection only when appropriate.

## Elder Brother

She teaches him patience through garden work and expects respectful conduct near flower beds.

## Younger Daughter

Younger Daughter is naturally fascinated by flowers. Lakshmi Paati welcomes her curiosity, offers safe tiny tasks, and teaches gentle hands.

## Family Dog

The German Shepherd recognizes her as a familiar neighbour. He does not run through her flower beds or disturb garland work.

---

# Voice and Body Language

Voice:

* Soft, clear, mature Tamil cadence.
* Patient pace.
* Warm small laugh.
* Calm tone even when redirecting a child.

Body language:

* Gentle flower-picking movements.
* Small precise threading motions.
* A soft nod instead of frequent instruction.
* Relaxed seated posture during garland-making.

Signature sounds:

* Flower stems rustling.
* Thread pulling gently through blossoms.
* Basket set on thinnai.
* Quiet voice during temple preparation.

---

# Daily Rhythm

She generally:

* Begins with light prayer and morning garden inspection.
* Picks flowers before heat affects them.
* Makes garlands or sorts blooms.
* Rests during midday heat.
* Waters or checks plants in cooler evening light.
* Prepares flowers for local prayer or festival needs when appropriate.

She has an independent rhythm and does not need to appear daily at the Main Family house.

---

# Expressions

Maintain:

* Gentle flower-garden smile.
* Focused concentration while threading.
* Quiet delight when a child notices a bloom.
* Calm concern for damaged plants after heavy rain.
* Soft reflective expression during prayer preparation.

Avoid exaggerated sorrow, mystical trance, or comic elder expressions.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Age 65–72 range.
* Lakshmi Paati / Widow Aunty identity.
* House N-04 residence.
* Independent elder household status.
* Jasmine, flower garden, and garland-making association.
* Gentle dignified behaviour.

Never:

* Change her into a young widow.
* Remove the flower garden.
* Make widowhood her sole narrative function.
* Use her as a magical remedy source.
* Turn her into a dependent background figure.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Lakshmi Paati, the Widow Aunty, within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Lakshmi Paati should feel like jasmine at dawn: quiet, familiar, carefully tended, and deeply woven into the village's faith and memory.
