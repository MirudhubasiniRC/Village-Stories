# Neighbour Family 03 — Lakshmi Paati's Household

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical documentation for Neighbour Family 03: the widow aunty household at House N-04, southwest of the Main Family.

The Village Neighbourhood map already identifies this resident as Lakshmi Paati. That established identity remains permanent; this folder provides the production-ready functional role of Widow Aunty.

---

# Household Identity

Neighbour Family 03 is a one-person household led by Lakshmi Paati, a widowed elder known throughout the neighbourhood for her flower garden, jasmine, careful garland-making, and gentle presence.

Although the initial role concept described a widow aunty aged 45–55, the existing map canon uses “Lakshmi Paati.” In Tamil village use, this establishes her as an elder. Her locked production age is therefore **65–72**.

She lives independently. Neighbours offer ordinary care and companionship, while her dignity and self-sufficiency remain central.

# Relationship With Main Family

She:

* Shares jasmine, flowers, or small garlands with Mother when natural.
* Treats Elder Brother and Younger Daughter with gentle elder affection.
* Appears in temple, garden, festival, and quiet-neighbour stories.
* Receives ordinary respectful help from Father and neighbours when a task exceeds her strength.
* Knows the Family Dog as a calm, familiar visitor.

She is never portrayed as dependent on the Main Family for every need.

Always maintain:

* House N-04 / Lakshmi Paati map continuity.
* Independent elder-widow household identity.
* Permanent flower garden and garland association.
* Gentle, dignified relationship with neighbours.

* Alter her into a younger 45–55-year-old woman.
* Treat widowhood as constant sadness.
* Make her helpless, mystical, or a source of melodrama.
