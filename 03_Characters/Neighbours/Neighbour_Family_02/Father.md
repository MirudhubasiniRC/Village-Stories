# Neighbour Father

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Father of Neighbour Family 02 throughout the Village Stories universe.

He is a recurring young farmer living at House N-03, the Kumar Family home northeast of the Main Family.

---

# Identity

The Neighbour Father is approximately **32–36 years old**.

He is a capable rural Tamil Nadu farmer, a respected friend of Father, and an active member of the village's shared seasonal-work culture.

He is not a visual or personality duplicate of Father. His identity is defined by cooperative steadiness, approachable humour, and his own family responsibilities.

---

# Narrative Role

He represents:

* Farming cooperation.
* Neighbourly friendship.
* Shared harvest effort.
* Practical village resilience.

---

# Appearance

Maintain:

* Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion.
* Healthy lean working build.
* Medium height, approximately 167–174 cm.
* Dark brown eyes.
* Naturally black short hair, practical and slightly sun-textured.
* A neat modest moustache is acceptable and preferred if established in first locked visuals.
* Strong practical hands and forearms from field labour.

He should be visually distinct from Father through face shape, hairline, smile, and body proportion while sharing the authenticity of a working farmer.

---

# Clothing

Everyday:

* Simple cotton shirt, usually half-sleeved for work.
* Traditional cotton veshti / dhoti in a practical wrap.
* Thundu over shoulder when useful.
* Bare feet in compound or traditional sandals on paths and fields as needed.

Festival:

* Fresh clean shirt and white or cream veshti.
* No modern fashion, designer styling, or gym aesthetic.

---

# Personality

He is:

* Reliable.
* Cooperative.
* Cheerful in a restrained way.
* Practical.
* Respectful.
* Patient with his son.
* Comfortable asking for or offering help.

He has slightly more easy social warmth than Father's quieter reserve, but remains grounded.

---

# Skills

He naturally knows:

* Seasonal field labour.
* Shared irrigation work.
* Harvest cooperation.
* Basic livestock and tool care.
* Carrying and organizing farm loads.
* Reading practical weather signs.

He is a capable farmer, never a superhuman labour hero.

---

# Behaviour

He naturally:

* Greets Father as a working equal.
* Joins shared work when a task needs extra hands.
* Checks on neighbours after heavy rain or a difficult day.
* Returns home to his own family rhythm.
* Allows children to observe and help only in age-appropriate ways.

His help is freely offered, not a debt the Main Family owes.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

## Father

They are friends and farming peers. They may share tools, labour, weather concern, and quiet satisfaction after work.

## Mother

He greets Mother respectfully and recognizes her household expertise without intruding.

## Elder Brother and Younger Daughter

He is a trusted familiar adult. He treats both children gently and never asks them to do unsafe work.

## Family Dog

The German Shepherd recognizes him as a known neighbour and remains calm around him.

---

# Voice and Body Language

Voice:

* Warm, medium rural Tamil voice.
* Slightly more openly cheerful than Father's.
* Practical and respectful.

Body language:

* Easy working stance.
* Open palm when offering help.
* Natural shoulder-thundu adjustment.
* Calm tool and field movements.

---

# Daily Rhythm

He follows a farming day:

* Early outdoor start.
* Field and seasonal work.
* Midday return or rest.
* Afternoon labour as weather allows.
* Evening return to House N-03 and family.

He may work beside Father during specific shared tasks, but he has an independent routine.

---

# Expressions

Maintain:

* Easy greeting smile.
* Focused field concentration.
* Quiet concern in bad weather.
* Warm pride when his son learns.
* Simple relief after shared work is complete.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Age 32–36 range.
* Young farming-father identity.
* Cooperative friendship with Father.
* Visually distinct face and silhouette from Father.
* House N-03 residence.

Never:

* Make him identical to Father.
* Create rivalry as a default dynamic.
* Use modern farming machinery or fashion styling without future canon.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Neighbour Father within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Neighbour Father should feel like the farmer who arrives with an extra pair of hands before anyone has to ask.
