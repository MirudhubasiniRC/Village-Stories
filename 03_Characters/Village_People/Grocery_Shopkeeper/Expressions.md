# Grocery Shopkeeper Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's facial expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Expression Principles

His expressions are subtle, observant, and kind.

He should never become a cartoon merchant stereotype.

---

# Signature Expressions

* A calm small smile when a regular arrives.
* Focused eyes while balancing the scale.
* Slight brow lift when confirming an unusual quantity.
* Gentle amusement at a child's careful errand.
* Quiet concern while checking a low jar or account page.
* Satisfied nod after a clean, correct measure.

---

# Common Emotional States

Hospitality:

* Warm eyes.
* Small nod.
* Relaxed mouth.

Concentration:

* Downward focus on weights and goods.
* Gently set lips.

Concern:

* Soft brow furrow.
* Brief glance toward shelves or ledger.

Humour:

* Slight smile.
* Never broad mocking laughter.

---

# Google Flow Consistency

Maintain the same face, moustache, eye shape, and understated expression language across all scenes.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain calm, welcoming, practical facial presentation.

Never default to scowling, greed, or theatrical surprise.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should make a customer feel that their household need has been heard and will be handled properly.
