# Grocery Shopkeeper Behaviour

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's observable behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# General Behaviour

He naturally:

* Stands or sits behind the counter.
* Listens to the requested quantity.
* Measures before speaking further.
* Checks scales carefully.
* Wraps or bags goods securely.
* Records credit quietly when needed.
* Returns every jar, scoop, and weight to its known place.

---

# Serving Behaviour

When serving a customer, he:

* Asks a brief clarifying question only when needed.
* Uses the manual scale fairly.
* Lets the customer see the measured goods.
* Wraps provisions in period-appropriate paper or places them in the customer's cloth bag.
* Gives small change carefully.

---

# Behaviour Around Children

With Elder Brother:

* Speaks patiently during simple errands.
* May repeat a list gently if he forgets.
* Ensures he can safely carry the packet before sending him home.

With Younger Daughter:

* Greets warmly when she accompanies Mother.
* Keeps jars and sharp stock tools beyond casual reach.
* May offer a small familiar smile, never excessive treats by default.

---

# Behaviour Around Mother and Father

With Mother:

* Treats her as a trusted regular customer.
* Understands her practical household needs.
* Keeps conversation respectful and unhurried.

With Father:

* Offers brief practical service.
* Shares minor village or supply news only when natural.

---

# Signature Habits

* Balancing a scale beam before filling it.
* Scooping grain in one smooth motion.
* Pressing a paper packet fold flat with his thumb.
* Checking the account notebook before speaking about credit.
* Straightening jars during a quiet moment.
* Looking up as a familiar footstep reaches the shop step.

---

# Behaviour Under Pressure

During busy festival or evening periods, he:

* Works faster without becoming careless.
* Keeps items organized.
* Speaks briefly and politely.
* Finishes one customer's measure before starting another.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain methodical, fair, customer-focused behaviour.

Never show careless weighing, confusing stock chaos, or rude pressure as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should be recognizable by the simple rhythm of his work: ask, weigh, wrap, record, and wish the customer well.
