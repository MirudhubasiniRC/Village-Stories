# Grocery Shopkeeper Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical physical appearance of the Grocery Shopkeeper throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Identity

He is a healthy middle-aged South Indian village man shaped by years of standing behind a small shop counter, carrying sacks, measuring grain, and greeting neighbours.

He should appear practical, attentive, and trustworthy—never glamorous.

---

# Estimated Age

Approximately:

**45–52 years old**

He is clearly older than Father and close in generation to the Tea Stall Owner, while retaining a distinct identity.

---

# Height and Build

Maintain:

* Approximately 165–172 cm.
* Medium, settled build.
* Strong hands and forearms from sacks, scales, and stock handling.
* Slight middle-aged softness is natural.
* Never a gym physique or exaggerated frailty.

---

# Skin Tone

Maintain:

* Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion.
* Natural texture.
* Slight sunlight on face and arms from the open shopfront.

---

# Face and Features

Maintain:

* Calm oval or gently rectangular face.
* Dark brown observant eyes.
* Natural brows.
* A neat modest traditional moustache is preferred as a permanent distinguishing feature.
* Gentle lines around eyes from customer interaction.

His face should communicate patient attention rather than sternness.

---

# Hair

Maintain:

* Short, naturally black hair with visible early grey at temples.
* Simple rural grooming.
* No modern salon cut or fashion styling.

---

# Hands

Hands should appear:

* Skilled with scales and paper packets.
* Clean but lightly dusted by grain or spice during work.
* Capable of lifting stock without exaggerated strength.

---

# Recognizable Features

He should remain instantly recognizable through:

* Neat traditional moustache.
* Early grey at temples.
* Dark observant eyes.
* Medium settled build.
* Counter-and-scale silhouette.
* Calm familiar expression.

---

# Environmental Appearance

During work:

* A trace of grain dust on fingers or shirt.
* Soft spice colour on a scoop or palm when appropriate.
* Dim rear-shelf shadow behind him.

These are temporary work details, not permanent redesigns.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain the same face, age range, hair, moustache, build, and shopkeeper silhouette.

Never:

* Turn him into a younger duplicate of Father.
* Give him modern business styling.
* Replace him with a visually unrelated shop owner between episodes.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's physical appearance throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should look like the same familiar man who has weighed the village's rice and sugar for years.
