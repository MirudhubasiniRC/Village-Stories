# Grocery Shopkeeper

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete canonical documentation for the Grocery Shopkeeper, a recurring village character in the Village Stories universe.

He operates the permanent Small Grocery Store west of the Village Centre and appears in household-provision, errand, and neighbour-interaction stories.

Every image prompt, animation, storyboard, and story involving the Grocery Shopkeeper should reference this documentation.

---

# Character Role

The Grocery Shopkeeper is the reliable keeper of the village's daily provisions.

He measures rice and lentils, wraps spices, maintains the account notebook, and remembers the needs of familiar households.

He is a permanent public-facing village presence, not a member of the central family.

---

# Narrative Function

The Grocery Shopkeeper represents:

* Everyday trust.
* Honest small trade.
* Household continuity.
* Quiet neighbourly conversation.
* The practical link between home needs and village life.

The Grocery Shopkeeper is trust measured by the kilo.

Through his scale, account book, familiar greeting, and carefully wrapped provisions, he makes the Small Grocery Store feel like an essential part of village home life.
