# Grocery Shopkeeper Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's practical abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Skills

* Manual weighing and measure conversion.
* Rice, lentil, spice, sugar, and oil stock handling.
* Paper packet folding and wrapping.
* Shelf and jar organization.
* Credit account keeping in a handwritten notebook.
* Stock-condition observation.
* Small-change handling.
* Household-provision advice within his practical knowledge.

---

# Measurement Skill

He understands:

* Correct use of the balance scale.
* Standard weights.
* Small measured quantities for daily household needs.
* Preventing spills and wastage.
* Fair visible measurement.

Accuracy is a defining part of his craft.

---

# Stock Knowledge

He naturally knows:

* Which staples run low before festivals.
* How damp weather affects dry goods.
* Which items Mother or neighbours commonly need.
* When old stock needs rotation.
* How to protect goods from dust, moisture, and pests.

---

# Social Skill

He can:

* Explain a simple quantity to children.
* Remember trusted household credit without public embarrassment.
* Keep a queue calm during busy hours.
* Give direct, practical answers.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* A supermarket operator.
* A banker.
* A travelling wholesaler.
* A specialist doctor or herbal authority.

His knowledge belongs to his small village shop.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain high competence with scales, stock, wrapping, and accounts.

Never give him anachronistic barcode, calculator, or digital-inventory skills.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is honest precision—the right amount, wrapped properly, remembered fairly.
