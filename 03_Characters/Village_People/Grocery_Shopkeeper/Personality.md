# Grocery Shopkeeper Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Honest.
* Patient.
* Practical.
* Observant.
* Mildly reserved.
* Warm once conversation begins.
* Careful with detail.
* Respectful.

---

# Temperament

Maintain:

* Even pace.
* Quiet confidence.
* Calm response to requests.
* Gentle humour with known customers.

He never creates dramatic conflict around ordinary buying.

---

# Relationship With Work

He takes pride in:

* Correct weights.
* Clean jars.
* Dry, safe grain.
* Clear accounts.
* Not letting a regular household down when help is possible.

Work is service and responsibility, not merely sales.

---

# Strengths

* Trustworthiness.
* Detail memory.
* Patience with small errands.
* Practical household knowledge.
* Fair judgment.

---

# Weaknesses

He may:

* Become absorbed in his account book.
* Worry silently about unsettled credit.
* Prefer familiar routines over sudden supply changes.

---

# Sense of Humor

His humour is dry, soft, and brief.

He may smile at a child carefully remembering a list or gently tease a regular about forgetting salt again.

He never embarrasses customers.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain honesty, patience, accuracy, and understated warmth.

Never make him greedy, mocking, or scheming.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His personality should feel like a level scale: steady, fair, and dependable.
