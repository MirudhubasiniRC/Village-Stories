# Grocery Shopkeeper Continuity

## Purpose

This document acts as the final continuity checklist for the Grocery Shopkeeper.

---

# Permanent Identity

Always preserve:

* Recurring Grocery Shopkeeper.
* Early-1990s rural Tamil Nadu village man.
* Permanent operator of the Small Grocery Store west of Village Centre.
* Age approximately 45–52.

---

# Permanent Appearance

Always preserve:

* Warm medium-brown complexion.
* Medium settled build.
* Short black hair with early grey at temples.
* Neat modest traditional moustache.
* Dark observant eyes.
* Skilled scale-and-packet hands.

---

# Permanent Clothing

Always preserve:

* Light cotton shirt.
* Practical cotton veshti.
* Optional thundu.
* Humble period-correct appearance.

---

# Permanent Personality and Behaviour

Always preserve:

* Honesty.
* Accuracy.
* Patience.
* Quiet warmth.
* Fair visible weighing.
* Careful account habits.
* Respectful customer service.

---

# Permanent Location Lock

He is visually and narratively tied to:

```text
02_World/Locations/Small_Grocery_Store.md
```

Do not relocate his shop.

---

# Permanent Relationships

Always preserve:

* Trusted regular relationship with Mother.
* Respectful familiarity with Father.
* Patient guidance for Elder Brother's errands.
* Safe kindness around Younger Daughter.
* Courteous service to neighbours and elders.

---

# Never List

Never:

* Make him greedy or dishonest by default.
* Turn him into a modern supermarket cashier.
* Add barcode scanners, digital payments, or modern retail visuals.
* Replace manual scale, ledger, and period-appropriate wrapping.

---

# AI Consistency Rule

Google Flow must always generate the same man at the same Small Grocery Store.

Do not alter his face, age range, moustache, clothing language, or customer manner between episodes.

---

# Canon Authority

This document is the final continuity reference for the Grocery Shopkeeper.

---

# Guiding Principle

No matter how many household errands occur, he should always feel like the same fair, familiar man behind the same scale.
