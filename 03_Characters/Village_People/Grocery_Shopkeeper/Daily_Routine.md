# Grocery Shopkeeper Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's typical daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning shop opening

↓

First household customers

↓

Shelf / stock management

↓

Midday lull

↓

Afternoon errands and small purchases

↓

Evening provisions rush

↓

Account check and shutter closing
```

---

# Morning

He usually:

* Opens the shutter.
* Sweeps the step and entrance.
* Checks jars, scale, weights, and shelf order.
* Places frequently used goods within reach.
* Serves the first household customers.

---

# Mid-Morning

* Measures regular staples.
* Refills jars and shelf gaps.
* Receives small errands from children.
* Holds light neighbour conversation while working.

---

# Midday

* Customer flow slows.
* He checks stock, protects dry goods, and eats a simple meal when possible.
* The shop remains open or lightly watched according to the day's practical rhythm.

---

# Afternoon

* Neighbours pass for small needs.
* School-related pencil or notebook purchases may occur.
* Mother-type household buyers may complete errands before evening.

---

# Evening

* Families restock for dinner and the next morning.
* Father may stop for a practical item.
* Elder Brother may complete a supervised or clearly instructed errand.
* The shop becomes gently sociable at the step.

---

# Closing

He:

* Counts remaining loose change.
* Checks the account notebook.
* Covers or secures jars and stock.
* Lowers the shutter.
* Leaves the west-side path quiet.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer:

* More dust at the threshold.
* Greater care for food storage.

Monsoon:

* Damp-footprint management.
* Extra protection for dry goods.

Festival periods:

* Longer queues.
* Extra sugar, provisions, incense, and household demand.
* Later but still modest closing when required.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain regular opening, measured service, evening household rush, and secure closing.

Never leave the shop randomly unattended in active hours without narrative reason.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His day should feel like the practical rhythm behind many village meals—opening shelves in the morning and closing them only after homes have what they need.
