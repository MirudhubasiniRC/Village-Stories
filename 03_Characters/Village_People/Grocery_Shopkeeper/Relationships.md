# Grocery Shopkeeper Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Relationship With Mother

Mother is a trusted regular customer.

The Grocery Shopkeeper:

* Recognizes her household needs.
* Serves her respectfully.
* Lets her inspect or confirm a measure naturally.
* Maintains dignified privacy if she uses trusted household credit.

Their relationship is practical, warm, and rooted in years of village familiarity.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is a familiar customer who comes for practical necessities when passing.

Their conversation remains brief and respectful, often about supplies, rain, or village news.

---

# Relationship With Elder Brother

Elder Brother may be sent on small household errands.

The Grocery Shopkeeper:

* Gives clear packets.
* Repeats the order if needed.
* Encourages careful handling without treating him as an adult.

This can become an important small lesson in responsibility.

---

# Relationship With Younger Daughter

Younger Daughter usually accompanies Mother.

He treats her kindly, respects her curiosity, and keeps the work area safe.

She may watch the scale or sweet jars with wonder, but he does not become an exaggerated candy-giver.

---

# Relationship With Neighbours and Elders

He shares dependable, respectful relationships with regular customers.

The raised shop step naturally becomes a brief place for trusted conversation.

Elders receive particular patience and courtesy.

---

# Relationship With Other Village Traders

He respects the Tea Stall Owner and other workers as fellow contributors to village life.

They may exchange brief news, but their businesses remain distinct.

---

# Relationship With His Store

The Small Grocery Store is his livelihood, memory system, and public identity.

He treats its shelves, jars, scale, and notebook with care.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Trusted regular-customer relationships.
* Particular respect for Mother and elders.
* Patient guidance for Elder Brother.
* Safe gentle conduct around Younger Daughter.

Never create exploitative or hostile customer relationships as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's relationships throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships are built in small quantities over years: one familiar order, one remembered household, one fair measure at a time.
