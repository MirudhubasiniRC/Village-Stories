# Grocery Shopkeeper Identity

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical identity of the Grocery Shopkeeper within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Identity

The Grocery Shopkeeper is a recurring village man in rural Tamil Nadu during the early 1990s.

He owns or responsibly operates the Small Grocery Store west of the Village Centre. His work is based on fair measurement, memory, careful stock keeping, and long-standing local trust.

He knows customers by their household needs rather than treating them as anonymous sales.

---

# Narrative Role

He represents:

* Honest village commerce.
* Everyday provisioning.
* Dependable memory.
* Quiet community trust.
* Practical help.

---

# Life Role

He naturally:

* Opens and secures the shop.
* Measures and wraps provisions.
* Maintains shelves, jars, sacks, and tins.
* Keeps a simple credit notebook.
* Helps customers remember household basics.
* Watches the west-side path while working.

---

# Personal Values

* Fairness.
* Accuracy.
* Clean stock keeping.
* Trust.
* Respect for customers.
* Quiet hard work.

---

# Strengths

* Accurate hand measurement.
* Strong memory for goods and regular households.
* Calm customer service.
* Practical stock organization.
* Honest account keeping.

---

# Limitations

He may:

* Worry about delayed deliveries or low stock.
* Spend too long balancing his accounts after closing.
* Become quietly concerned when customers' credit runs long.

These concerns remain humane and never make him exploitative.

---

# Story Function

He naturally supports stories involving:

* Mother's household purchases.
* Elder Brother's small errand.
* Younger Daughter's curiosity about jars and scales.
* Festival provisions.
* A missing coin, wrong packet, or honest correction.
* Village conversation at the shop step.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Permanent link to the Small Grocery Store.
* Honest, measured, respectful trade.
* Early-1990s village shopkeeper identity.

Never:

* Turn him into a greedy merchant stereotype.
* Make him a supermarket manager.
* Detach him from his shop without a story reason.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Grocery Shopkeeper's identity within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He is not merely a seller of goods. He is the steady person who makes sure ordinary village homes can continue their ordinary days.
