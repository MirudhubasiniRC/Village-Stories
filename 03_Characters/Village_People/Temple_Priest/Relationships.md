# Temple Priest Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

## Mother

Mother brings flowers, offerings, or family prayer moments when natural. The Priest receives her respectfully and recognizes her care for tradition.

## Father

Father greets him with quiet respect during shrine visits or festival preparation.

## Elder Brother and Younger Daughter

He treats both children with kindness. He explains simple respectful behaviour without demanding perfection or fear.

## Family Dog

The German Shepherd remains outside the ritual space and behaves calmly nearby when accompanying family.

---

# Relationship With Lakshmi Paati

Lakshmi Paati's flowers and garlands naturally support shrine care. Their relationship is respectful, practical, and centred on flowers, offerings, and festival preparation.

---

# Relationship With Village

He welcomes villagers without favouritism.

He is respected, but does not control private family decisions or village governance.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain gentle spiritual hospitality and respectful boundaries.

Never use the Priest to impose fear, judgment, or social control.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's relationships.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships should make the shrine feel open to the village, not owned by one person.
