# Temple Priest Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's personality.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Calm.
* Gentle.
* Patient.
* Humble.
* Observant.
* Respectful.
* Quietly warm.

---

# Spiritual Philosophy

He believes devotion is shown through small repeated care: lighting a lamp, keeping the shrine clean, offering flowers, and meeting others with kindness.

He does not force belief or preach constantly.

---

# Strengths

* Ritual consistency.
* Calm presence.
* Cultural knowledge.
* Gentle listening.
* Festival organization support.

---

# Limitations

He may:

* Worry when rain, wind, or shortage threatens shrine preparation.
* Carry festival duties through fatigue.
* Prefer familiar ritual order over sudden change.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain humility and gentle spirituality.

Never make him superstitious, manipulative, frightening, or comic.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's personality.

---

# Guiding Principle

His faith should be visible in care, not performance.
