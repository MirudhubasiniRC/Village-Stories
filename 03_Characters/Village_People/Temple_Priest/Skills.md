# Temple Priest Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's practical and ritual abilities.

---

# Core Skills

* Daily Vinayagar pooja procedure.
* Brass lamp care.
* Flower and garland arrangement.
* Incense and offering preparation.
* Festival shrine decoration support.
* Ritual timing awareness.
* Gentle explanation of simple customs to children.

---

# Cultural Knowledge

He understands:

* Appropriate shrine cleanliness.
* Common offerings.
* Temple-festival preparation.
* When rituals require quiet and when community participation is welcome.

He does not claim knowledge outside his role or replace medical, legal, or practical village expertise.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain humble ritual competence and flower/lamp care.

Never portray supernatural powers or modern event-management technology.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's skills.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is preserving the small rituals that let the whole village feel connected.
