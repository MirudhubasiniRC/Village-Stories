# Temple Priest Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's facial expressions.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Peaceful resting face.
* Soft acknowledgement smile.
* Focused pooja concentration.
* Gentle attention toward children.
* Quiet concern when rain or wind affects shrine care.
* Warm festival-day satisfaction.

---

# Expression Principles

Maintain:

* Calm.
* Sincere.
* Understated.
* Never mystically exaggerated.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always keep the same face, age language, vibhuti detail during service, and gentle expression style.

Never use threatening stares, trances, or theatrical emotional extremes.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Temple Priest's expressions.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should make a visitor lower their voice naturally, not out of fear but out of peace.
