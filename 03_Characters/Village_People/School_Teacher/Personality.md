# School Teacher Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

She is:

* Kind.
* Patient.
* Encouraging.
* Fair.
* Clear-minded.
* Observant.
* Calmly disciplined.
* Respectful.

---

# Teaching Philosophy

She believes:

* Questions deserve attention.
* Practice matters more than embarrassment.
* Children learn at different speeds.
* Discipline should guide rather than humiliate.
* Village knowledge and school learning can respect one another.

---

# Strengths

* Explaining difficult basics simply.
* Helping shy children speak.
* Noticing effort.
* Keeping order without fear.
* Balancing warmth and standards.

---

# Weaknesses

She may:

* Worry about children who miss school.
* Become quietly frustrated by preventable lack of materials.
* Carry a pupil's problem home in her thoughts.

---

# Sense of Humor

Her humour is warm and light. She smiles at honest child mistakes, then helps correct them.

She never uses humour to shame a pupil.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain patience, fairness, clarity, and encouraging authority.

Never make her harsh, mocking, aloof, or unrealistically perfect.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her authority should make children feel safe enough to try again.
