# School Teacher Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Her clothing communicates practical professional dignity, traditional village life, and early-1990s authenticity.

---

# Everyday School Clothing

Maintain:

* Clean cotton or handloom cotton saree.
* Simple short-sleeved blouse.
* Comfortable practical drape for standing, writing, and moving between pupils.
* Small bindi.
* Minimal traditional earrings.
* Simple sandals outside; footwear removed only where culturally natural.

---

# Colour Palette

Preferred:

* Cream.
* Soft blue.
* Muted green.
* Maroon.
* Mustard.
* Faded rose.
* Subtle checks or narrow woven borders.

Avoid neon colours, designer sarees, heavy embroidery, and fashion-forward silhouettes.

---

# Accessories

Acceptable:

* Simple cloth bag for notebooks.
* Chalk or red pen stored practically.
* Modest thin watch only if permanently established in future visual canon.

Never use luxury handbags, heavy jewellery, or modern office accessories as defaults.

---

# Festival Clothing

* Cleaner handloom or silk-cotton saree.
* Fresh flowers if appropriate.
* Still modest and teacher-like.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional cotton teacher sarees, modest accessories, and period-correct styling.

Never:

* Add modern formal suits.
* Add jeans, branded clothing, or high-fashion presentation.
* Use a uniform absent from school canon.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her clothing should look ready for a morning assembly, a chalkboard lesson, and the walk home through the village.
