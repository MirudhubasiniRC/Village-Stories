# School Teacher

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical documentation for the School Teacher, a recurring village character in the Village Stories universe.

She teaches at the permanent Primary School and lives with her household at House N-05, southeast of the Main Family.

---

# Character Role

The School Teacher is a kind, patient primary-school teacher who brings structure, encouragement, Tamil, English, and mathematics learning into village childhood.

She is a respected village professional, not a strict caricature or a modern urban teacher placed into a rural setting.

---

# Narrative Function

She represents:

* Learning.
* Patient encouragement.
* Respectful discipline.
* School-day routine.
* A child's expanding world beyond home.
