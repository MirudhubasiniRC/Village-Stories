# School Teacher Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's typical daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning household preparation

↓

Walk to Primary School

↓

Assembly and lessons

↓

Midday school rhythm

↓

Afternoon classes and closing bell

↓

Walk home / light preparation

↓

Evening rest and next-day planning
```

---

# Morning

She generally:

* Completes simple household and prayer routines at House N-05.
* Prepares school materials.
* Walks to the Primary School before children fully gather.
* Helps ready the classroom and morning assembly rhythm.

---

# School Day

* Leads or supports assembly.
* Teaches primary lessons.
* Checks notebooks and slates.
* Supervises breaks in an age-appropriate way.
* Notices attendance and student wellbeing.
* Sends children home at the closing bell.

---

# Afternoon and Evening

She:

* Returns to House N-05.
* Completes light personal and household tasks.
* Reviews simple lesson material or notebooks when necessary.
* Rests without turning every evening into work.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer:

* More shade and water awareness at school.

Monsoon:

* Rain sounds on roof.
* Damp-path attendance sensitivity.
* More veranda or classroom-contained breaks.

Festival periods:

* School cultural activities when appropriate.
* Respect for family and community rhythms.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain school-day rhythm, classroom presence, and House N-05 return.

Never keep her at school or in the Main Family home without story reason.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her day should connect the quiet home morning to the school bell, then return her to the same village she helps children understand.
