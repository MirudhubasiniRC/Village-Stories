# School Teacher Identity

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical identity of the School Teacher within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Identity

The School Teacher is a recurring South Indian village woman, approximately **32–38 years old**, who teaches primary classes at the village Primary School.

She lives at House N-05, the established School Teacher's Family home southeast of the Main Family.

She believes children learn best when they are treated with patience, clarity, and genuine expectation.

---

# Narrative Role

She represents:

* Education rooted in community.
* Respectful classroom leadership.
* Encouragement for children.
* The connection between home life and formal learning.

---

# Responsibilities

She naturally:

* Teaches Tamil, English, and mathematics at primary level.
* Leads or supports morning assembly.
* Keeps classroom routines steady.
* Gives age-appropriate homework.
* Notices when a child needs extra encouragement.
* Communicates respectfully with parents when appropriate.

---

# Personal Values

* Patience.
* Fairness.
* Curiosity.
* Clear effort.
* Respect for children.
* Respect for parents and village culture.
* Learning through practice.

---

# Strengths

* Clear explanation.
* Gentle discipline.
* Classroom observation.
* Encouraging shy children.
* Practical organization.
* Strong memory for student progress.

---

# Limitations

She may:

* Carry school responsibilities home in her thoughts.
* Feel concerned when attendance drops during difficult seasonal periods.
* Become quietly tired after a full school day and community duties.

She is human, never a perfect teaching machine.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Female primary-school teacher identity.
* Age 32–38 range.
* Kind, patient, encouraging authority.
* Primary School and House N-05 location lock.

Never:

* Make her cruel, humiliating, or indifferent.
* Treat her as a modern corporate educator.
* Reduce her to only school exposition.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's identity within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

She should feel like the teacher a village remembers for making children believe they could learn.
