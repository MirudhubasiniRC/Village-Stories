# School Teacher Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's facial expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Warm small smile while greeting pupils.
* Attentive eyes while listening to an answer.
* Focused quiet concentration while writing on the blackboard.
* Patient pause for a shy child.
* Gentle satisfaction when a lesson lands.
* Mild concerned brow when a child is absent or upset.

---

# Expression Principles

Maintain:

* Calm.
* Encouraging.
* Clear.
* Understated.
* Never cartoonishly strict or exaggeratedly sweet.

---

# Emotional States

Encouragement:

* Warm eyes.
* Slight nod.
* Open expression.

Correction:

* Firmer but kind gaze.
* Relaxed mouth.
* No anger performance.

Pride:

* Gentle smile.
* Brightened eyes.
* Small approving nod.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain the same face, eye shape, smile style, and teacher-like calm.

Never default to scowling, mockery, or theatrical surprise.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her face should tell a child: “Take your time. Try again.”
