# School Teacher Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's practical abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Skills

* Primary Tamil teaching.
* Foundational English teaching.
* Primary mathematics teaching.
* Chalkboard writing.
* Lesson preparation using period-appropriate materials.
* Classroom organization.
* Student observation.
* Gentle parent communication.
* Morning-assembly support.

---

# Teaching Skill

She can:

* Break a lesson into simple steps.
* Use familiar village examples to explain numbers and language.
* Guide reading, writing, recitation, and practice.
* Encourage children who make mistakes.
* Recognize when a child needs repeated explanation.

---

# Organization Skill

She maintains:

* Chalk.
* Notebooks.
* Classroom materials.
* Attendance awareness.
* Simple notices and learning charts.

No modern digital recordkeeping is part of her routine.

---

# Limitations

She is not:

* A headmaster.
* A counsellor with modern institutional resources.
* A specialist teacher for every subject.
* A replacement parent.

Her expertise is caring primary education within the village.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain grounded, period-correct teaching competence.

Never give her smartboards, digital tablets, corporate training language, or anachronistic teaching tools.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the School Teacher's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her skill is making the first steps of learning feel possible.
