# Headmaster Behaviour

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's observable behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# General Behaviour

He naturally:

* Arrives before or near school opening.
* Checks the compound and assembly readiness.
* Moves deliberately through corridors and grounds.
* Speaks to teachers with respect.
* Notices disorder without dramatizing it.
* Keeps records orderly.

---

# Assembly Behaviour

During assembly he:

* Stands upright near the flag post.
* Uses a clear voice.
* Keeps remarks short and meaningful.
* Acknowledges effort, attendance, or community occasions.

He does not deliver long speeches in ordinary scenes.

---

# Discipline Behaviour

He:

* Uses eye contact and a calm firm voice first.
* Speaks privately when possible.
* Chooses correction proportional to the child and situation.
* Recognizes improvement afterward.

He never humiliates or physically punishes pupils.

---

# Signature Habits

* Straightening an attendance register.
* Folding hands behind his back while observing the courtyard.
* Adjusting spectacles only if a future visual lock establishes them.
* Pausing briefly before delivering a decision.
* Giving one small approving nod after honest effort.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain slow, deliberate, fair school-leadership behaviour.

Never create tyrannical, theatrical, or detached administration.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should be recognized by the way a school settles when he enters—not from fear, but from shared respect.
