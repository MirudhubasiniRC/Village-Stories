# Headmaster Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Strict.
* Fair.
* Calm.
* Dignified.
* Observant.
* Patient beneath formality.
* Deeply committed to children learning.

---

# Authority Philosophy

He believes discipline protects learning.

His strictness is never anger for its own sake. He expects punctuality, care with school materials, and respectful conduct because he wants children to take their own futures seriously.

---

# Strengths

* Fair judgment.
* Public composure.
* Clear standards.
* Encouraging effort.
* Seeing potential in quiet children.

---

# Weaknesses

He may:

* Speak too formally when a child needs softer reassurance.
* Worry about school resources.
* Carry responsibility alone before asking for help.

---

# Sense of Humor

His humour is dry and rare but warm. A small smile from him should feel earned, not cold.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain firm kindness and measured authority.

Never make him cruel, easily angered, corrupt, or comically rigid.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His strictness should feel like a fence around a young plant: firm enough to protect growth, never meant to restrict it.
