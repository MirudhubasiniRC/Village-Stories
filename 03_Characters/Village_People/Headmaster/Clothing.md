# Headmaster Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

His clothes communicate humble educational authority in early-1990s rural Tamil Nadu.

They must be clean and dignified, never urban-corporate or fashionable.

---

# Everyday School Clothing

Maintain:

* Clean light-coloured cotton shirt.
* White or cream cotton veshti / dhoti.
* Simple leather or rubber sandals.
* Optional plain thundu for heat or travel.

The fit is practical and modest.

---

# Colour Palette

* White.
* Cream.
* Pale blue.
* Soft grey.
* Light beige.

Avoid dark power-suit imagery, bright neon clothing, logos, and designer fabrics.

---

# Formal Occasions

For Independence Day, annual day, parent meetings, or formal school functions:

* Freshly washed white veshti.
* Neater cotton or silk-cotton shirt.
* Same restrained grooming.

He does not transform into a modern official.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional shirt-and-veshti educational authority.

Never use suits, ties, branded clothing, or modern officewear.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His clothing should feel as steady as the school bell: simple, clean, and immediately understood.
