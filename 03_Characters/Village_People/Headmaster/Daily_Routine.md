# Headmaster Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's typical school-day routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning preparation

↓

Arrival at Primary School

↓

Assembly / opening checks

↓

Records, classroom rounds, school matters

↓

Midday oversight

↓

Afternoon administration and closing

↓

Return home
```

---

# Morning

He:

* Prepares simply at home.
* Reaches the school in time for opening.
* Checks assembly, staff readiness, and compound order.

---

# School Day

He:

* Oversees school operation.
* Visits classrooms when natural.
* Handles records and parent matters.
* Supports teachers without constantly interrupting lessons.
* Attends significant school concerns.

---

# Closing

He:

* Ensures the school day closes in order.
* Secures important records.
* Leaves the compound calm after children have gone home.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Monsoon requires attention to wet grounds and attendance.

Festival and national occasions require additional planning, assemblies, and cultural programs.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain a school-centred workday and orderly closing.

Never place him in unrelated village locations excessively without a story reason.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His routine should give the school its dependable spine from first bell to last.
