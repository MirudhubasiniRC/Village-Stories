# Headmaster Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's physical appearance throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Appearance

Maintain:

* Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion.
* Approximately 168–175 cm height.
* Lean-to-medium mature build.
* Calm rectangular or oval face.
* Dark brown attentive eyes.
* Short black hair with clear natural grey at temples.
* Neat full or modest traditional moustache, permanently consistent once visualized.
* Upright, unhurried posture.

He should look respectable and experienced, never stern by default or artificially youthful.

---

# Clothing

Everyday school clothing:

* Clean cotton shirt, usually light cream, white, pale blue, or soft grey.
* White or cream traditional veshti / dhoti.
* Simple sandals.
* Optional plain cotton shoulder cloth.

Formal school-day variation:

* Slightly neater shirt and veshti for assembly, Independence Day, or annual events.

Never use suit-and-tie corporate styling, designer fashion, or modern office aesthetics.

---

# Recognizable Features

He should remain instantly recognizable through:

* Grey-at-temples hair.
* Neat moustache.
* Upright school-authority posture.
* Clean light shirt and traditional veshti.
* Calm direct eyes.

---

# Environmental Appearance

At school:

* May hold attendance book, papers, or a simple file.
* May have faint chalk dust on fingers after visiting classrooms.
* Looks composed at the flag post or classroom corridor.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain the same age range, hair greying, moustache, school attire, and composed posture.

Never:

* Make him visually identical to Father.
* Add modern executive accessories.
* Change him into a harsh uniformed disciplinarian.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's physical appearance throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should look like the person who has stood at the school gate for many years, holding both order and hope in equal measure.
