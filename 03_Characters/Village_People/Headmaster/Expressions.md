# Headmaster Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's facial expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Calm neutral school-authority face.
* Small approving nod.
* Focused brow during records or serious matters.
* Brief warm smile when a child shows honest effort.
* Measured concern during a school difficulty.

---

# Expression Principles

Maintain:

* Understated.
* Dignified.
* Clear.
* Never threatening by default.

---

# Emotional States

Discipline:

* Direct steady eyes.
* Firm but controlled mouth.
* No rage.

Pride:

* Softened eyes.
* Small smile.
* Restrained approval.

Concern:

* Mild brow furrow.
* Quiet concentration.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain the same moustache, face, calm eye language, and restrained emotional presentation.

Never default to exaggerated scowling, shouting, or villain-like expressions.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should show that standards can be serious without being unkind.
