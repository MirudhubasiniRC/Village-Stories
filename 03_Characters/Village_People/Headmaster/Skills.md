# Headmaster Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's practical abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Skills

* Primary-school administration.
* Assembly leadership.
* Record and attendance management.
* Teacher coordination.
* Parent communication.
* Formal school-event organization.
* Clear public speaking.
* Fair conflict resolution within school context.

---

# Educational Skill

He understands:

* Primary-school learning needs.
* Classroom order.
* Age-appropriate expectations.
* The importance of attendance.
* How seasonal village work affects children and families.

He is not a substitute for the School Teacher's day-to-day classroom warmth and skill.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* A modern education administrator with digital systems.
* A specialist counsellor.
* A village political leader.
* A replacement father to pupils.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain period-correct school leadership and record-based administration.

Never introduce computers, digital registers, or corporate-school practices.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is making a modest school function with seriousness, fairness, and care.
