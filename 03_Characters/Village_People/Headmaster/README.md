# Headmaster

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical documentation for the Headmaster, a recurring village character in the Village Stories universe.

He is the respected senior school authority at the permanent Primary School.

---

# Character Role

The Headmaster maintains the school's standards, assemblies, records, public occasions, and sense of educational dignity.

He is strict when needed, but always fair, measured, and committed to children learning.

---

# Narrative Function

He represents:

* School responsibility.
* Fair authority.
* Community respect for education.
* Formal village occasions.
* Patient standards.
