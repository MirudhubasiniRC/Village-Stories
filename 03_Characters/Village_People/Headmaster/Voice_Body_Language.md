# Headmaster Voice & Body Language

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's voice, speech, body language, and signature sounds throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Voice

Maintain:

* Clear medium-low male Tamil voice.
* Measured pace.
* Firm assembly projection.
* Respectful conversational tone with teachers and parents.

He speaks loudly only when a public school moment requires it.

---

# Body Language

Maintain:

* Upright posture.
* Deliberate walking pace.
* Small precise hand gesture when making a point.
* Hands behind back while observing school grounds when natural.
* Stillness while listening to a child's explanation.

---

# Signature Gestures

* Closing an attendance register carefully.
* Straightening a paper stack.
* Giving one approving nod.
* Raising an open palm to settle assembly attention.
* Adjusting shirt cuff or veshti edge before formal remarks.

---

# Signature Sounds

* Clear assembly address.
* Paper and attendance-register movement.
* Steps on veranda.
* School bell nearby.
* A calm firm instruction.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain clear measured voice and restrained dignified movement.

Never use aggressive yelling, theatrical pacing, or corporate-presenter gestures.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's voice and body language throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should be recognizable by the calm voice that carries across assembly and the measured silence that follows.
