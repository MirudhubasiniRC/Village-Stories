# Headmaster Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Relationship With School Teacher

The School Teacher is a respected teaching colleague.

He:

* Sets school-wide standards.
* Trusts her daily classroom leadership.
* Coordinates professionally.
* Offers guidance without undermining her.

Their relationship is formal, respectful, and warm beneath professional structure.

---

# Relationship With Elder Brother

Elder Brother knows him as the senior school authority.

The Headmaster recognizes effort, expects respectful conduct, and encourages learning without becoming a feared figure.

---

# Relationship With Younger Daughter

He recognizes her as a younger neighbourhood child during school events or future readiness moments.

Their contact remains age-appropriate and brief.

---

# Relationship With Parents

He speaks respectfully with Mother, Father, and other parents about school matters.

He understands their farming and household responsibilities, while reinforcing the value of children's education.

---

# Relationship With Village

He is respected by elders and families as a school leader.

He does not dominate village civic or religious life outside his educational role.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain professional boundaries, fairness, and respectful parent communication.

Never turn him into an omnipotent village authority or a replacement parent.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Headmaster's relationships throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships should make the school feel respected by the village and accountable to it.
