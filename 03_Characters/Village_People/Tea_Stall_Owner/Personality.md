# Tea Stall Owner Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Tea Stall Owner's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

- Friendly
- Steady
- Practical
- Observant
- Mildly humorous
- Hospitable
- Hardworking
- Respectful
- Talkative in a useful village way
- Never cruel

---

# Temperament

Maintain:

- Warm readiness for customers.
- Calm under morning rush.
- Easy conversation.
- Quiet pride in good tea.

He rarely becomes angry.

If supplies run short, he becomes practical rather than dramatic.

---

# Sense of Humor

He enjoys light roadside humour.

He may tease regulars gently.

His jokes never humiliate.

---

# Strengths

- Consistency.
- Customer memory.
- Social ease.
- Reliable opening hours.
- Warm presence.

---

# Weaknesses

- May linger in talk during slow hours.
- May push through tiredness instead of resting.
- May worry quietly about business on rainy low-customer days.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain warm, hospitable, practical personality.

Never make him greedy, rude, or villainous.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Tea Stall Owner's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His personality should taste like the tea he serves—hot, familiar, and comforting.
