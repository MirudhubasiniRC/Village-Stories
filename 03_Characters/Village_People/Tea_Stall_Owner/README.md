# Tea Stall Owner

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete canonical documentation for the Tea Stall Owner, a recurring village character in the Village Stories universe.

He operates the permanent Tea Stall in the North-East and appears whenever morning tea, roadside conversation, farmer pauses, or traveller rest become part of a story.

Every image prompt, animation, storyboard, and story involving the Tea Stall Owner should reference this documentation.

---

# Character Role

The Tea Stall Owner is the living host of the Tea Stall.

He prepares tea and filter coffee, serves simple snacks, remembers regulars, and keeps the stall's warm social rhythm steady from morning opening to evening closing.

He is not a main family member.

He is a permanent village presence the audience should recognize instantly.

---

# Narrative Function

The Tea Stall Owner represents:

* Everyday hospitality.
* Village news exchange.
* Roadside warmth.
* Working-class steadiness.
* Gentle humour.
* Continuity of public life outside the family compound.
