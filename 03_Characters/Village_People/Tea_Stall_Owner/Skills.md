# Tea Stall Owner Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Tea Stall Owner's practical abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Skills

- Consistent tea brewing.
- Filter coffee preparation.
- Flame/stove control for stall cooking.
- Glass handling and quick washing.
- Simple snack preparation or stocking.
- Customer memory.
- Small-change handling and fair trade.
- Stall setup and closing.
- Weather-aware roadside management.

---

# Tea Expertise

He understands:

- Water boil timing.
- Milk balance.
- Tea strength.
- Sugar preference patterns.
- Serving temperature.
- Glass heat caution.

A good glass of tea is his signature craft.

---

# Social Skill

He can:

- Keep conversation light.
- Read whether a customer wants talk or quiet.
- Share news without creating conflict.
- Make travellers feel briefly at home.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

- A restaurant chef.
- A mechanic.
- A story hero with unrelated mastery.

His excellence stays rooted in the stall.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain high tea-stall competence.

Never portray him as newly incompetent at his own craft without strong temporary story reason.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Tea Stall Owner's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is the invisible perfection of ordinary service—the same comforting tea, day after day.
