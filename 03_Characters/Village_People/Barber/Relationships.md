# Barber Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's relationships.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is a familiar customer, especially before festivals or when ordinary grooming is due.

Their conversation remains practical, respectful, and lightly friendly.

---

# Relationship With Elder Brother

Elder Brother receives age-appropriate haircuts.

The Barber explains simply, keeps him calm, and never mocks restlessness.

---

# Relationship With Younger Daughter

Younger Daughter may accompany family but is not treated as a routine customer within this male barber-service canon.

She remains a safe curious observer only when appropriate.

---

# Relationship With Village

He knows many households and treats all customers discreetly. His work area allows gentle conversation, never malicious gossip.

---

# Relationship With Other Traders

He is a familiar west-side worker near the Grocery Store route, but his tree stand remains distinct from a permanent commercial shop.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain respect, privacy, and safe boundaries with every customer.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's relationships.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships are built through repeat trust: familiar chairs, familiar faces, and work done respectfully.
