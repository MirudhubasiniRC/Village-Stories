# Barber Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's practical abilities.

---

# Core Skills

* Traditional village haircutting.
* Child haircut patience.
* Simple moustache and beard trimming.
* Comb and scissors handling.
* Mirror and chair setup.
* Tool cleaning.
* Cloth and hygiene management.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* A modern fashion stylist.
* A medical professional.
* A beauty-parlour operator.
* A barber-shop owner with modern electric equipment.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always use modest, period-appropriate hand tools and practical grooming knowledge.

Never introduce modern electric salon systems as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's skills.

---

# Guiding Principle

His craft is simple precision: familiar tools, steady hands, and care for every customer.
