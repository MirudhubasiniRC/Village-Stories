# Barber Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's daily rhythm.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning setup beneath shade tree

↓

Daylight customer service

↓

Midday heat pause

↓

Afternoon work

↓

Tool cleaning and packing
```

---

# Morning

He:

* Checks the tree-side workspace.
* Wipes chair and mirror.
* Sets out clean cloth and tools.
* Receives early customers when natural.

---

# Day and Afternoon

* Provides haircuts by daylight.
* Pauses in harsh heat.
* Resumes as shade shifts.
* Serves more customers before festivals or school events.

---

# Closing

He:

* Cleans tools.
* Sweeps the work area.
* Packs the tool box.
* Leaves the tree/veranda clean and ordinary again.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Monsoon work pauses or uses temporary cover. Festival periods bring more grooming appointments. Summer shade determines work pace.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain daylight open-air service and careful clean packing.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's daily routine.

---

# Guiding Principle

His day follows the shade: opening under leaves, working with care, and closing before the village evening settles.
