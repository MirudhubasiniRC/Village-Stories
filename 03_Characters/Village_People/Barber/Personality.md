# Barber Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's personality.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Friendly.
* Patient.
* Precise.
* Mildly humorous.
* Discreet.
* Respectful.
* Calm with children.

---

# Professional Philosophy

He believes grooming should leave people feeling clean, comfortable, and respected.

Conversation is optional. He listens without spreading private matters as village gossip.

---

# Strengths

* Steady hands.
* Customer comfort.
* Child patience.
* Tool care.
* Festival-day organization.

---

# Weaknesses

He may tire during busy pre-festival days or worry when rain interrupts open-air work.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain warmth, discretion, and calm competence.

Never make him mocking, intrusive, or a gossip caricature.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's personality.

---

# Guiding Principle

His friendliness should be as light as the scissors: present, careful, never cutting too deep.
