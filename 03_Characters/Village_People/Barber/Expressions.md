# Barber Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's facial expressions.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Friendly greeting smile.
* Focused eyes while cutting.
* Gentle reassuring look toward a child.
* Mild amusement at harmless conversation.
* Weather-check concern when rain approaches.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain calm, attentive, understated expression.

Never make him mocking, overly theatrical, or constantly grinning.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's expressions.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should reassure a customer before the cloth is even draped.
