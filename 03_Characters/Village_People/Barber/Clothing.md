# Barber Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's wardrobe.

---

# Everyday Clothing

Maintain:

* Simple clean cotton shirt.
* Practical cotton veshti / dhoti.
* Soft thundu or clean cloth for work.
* Simple sandals near the tree stand.

Colours remain humble: white, cream, pale blue, beige, grey, or subtle checks.

---

# Work Adjustments

* Sleeves may be rolled.
* Veshti remains practical and secure.
* A clean barber cloth is used for customers, not worn as fashion.

---

# Festival Days

* Fresh clean shirt and veshti.
* More careful grooming of his own moustache and hair.
* Same modest village identity.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never use salon uniforms, modern stylist aprons, jeans, or branded clothing.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's clothing.

---

# Guiding Principle

His clothing should remain clean, simple, and ready for a day's careful work beneath the tree.
