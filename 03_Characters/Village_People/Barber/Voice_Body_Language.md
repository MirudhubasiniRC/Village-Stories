# Barber Voice & Body Language

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Barber's voice, body language, and signature sounds.

---

# Voice

Maintain:

* Warm medium male Tamil voice.
* Low conversational volume.
* Calm reassurance around children.
* Respectful greeting for elders.

---

# Body Language

Maintain:

* Balanced stance beside chair.
* Precise hand movement.
* Gentle head positioning only with customer consent and care.
* Relaxed waiting posture under tree shade.

---

# Signature Sounds

* Scissors snipping.
* Comb through hair.
* Barber cloth rustle.
* Tool box opening.
* Water vessel movement.
* Leaves moving overhead.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain soft voice and safe precise movements.

Never make him shout, perform exaggerated salon gestures, or use modern equipment.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Barber's voice and body language.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should be recognizable by scissors, shade, and a calm voice saying “sit still” with kindness.
