# Potter Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's facial expressions.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Quiet concentration while centring clay.
* Small satisfied smile when a vessel holds its shape.
* Calm weather concern when rain threatens drying pieces.
* Gentle attention toward children observing.
* Warm festival satisfaction around finished lamps.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain understated, patient expression.

Never use exaggerated mystical, comic, or modern-artist performance.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's expressions.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her face should show patience before pride.
