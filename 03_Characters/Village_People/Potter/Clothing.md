# Potter Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's wardrobe.

---

# Everyday Clothing

Maintain:

* Practical cotton saree with a secure work drape.
* Simple blouse.
* Earthy colours: clay red, cream, muted green, brown, faded blue, or mustard.
* Minimal jewellery.
* Bare feet near the clay wheel when practical; simple sandals on paths.

---

# Work Adjustments

* Saree edge may be tucked for wheel work.
* Clay marks are temporary and natural.
* Hair remains tied safely back.

---

# Festival Clothing

* Clean handloom or cotton saree.
* Fresh flowers only when not working around wet clay and kiln heat.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never use modern studio overalls, designer fashion, or heavy jewellery.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's clothing.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her clothes should feel made for earth, water, sun, and the steady turning of the wheel.
