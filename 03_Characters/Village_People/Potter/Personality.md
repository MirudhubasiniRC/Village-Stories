# Potter Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's personality.

---

# Core Personality

She is:

* Patient.
* Grounded.
* Quietly creative.
* Practical.
* Warm.
* Observant.
* Proud of durable work.

---

# Work Philosophy

She believes usefulness and beauty belong together: a water pot, cooking vessel, or small diya should serve real life and still carry the maker's care.

---

# Strengths

* Patience.
* Fine hand control.
* Kiln awareness.
* Calm teaching through demonstration.

---

# Weaknesses

She may worry when rain interrupts drying or when kiln firing risks a large batch.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never make her mystical, fragile, or a modern-art stereotype.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's personality.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her personality should feel like clay: patient, responsive, and strong once given time.
