# Potter Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's relationships.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

Mother is a natural customer for practical clay cooking and storage vessels when needed. Father may obtain practical pots or arrange repair/replacement for household use.

Elder Brother may observe wheel work from a safe distance. Younger Daughter may be fascinated by clay but only handles it under direct adult guidance and away from the kiln.

---

# Relationship With Temple Priest and Lakshmi Paati

Festival and shrine preparations may bring the Potter together with the Temple Priest and Lakshmi Paati through oil lamps, diyas, flowers, and ritual needs.

---

# Relationship With Village

She supplies useful everyday pottery fairly and is respected as a traditional artisan.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain practical, respectful artisan relationships and kiln-safety boundaries.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's relationships.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her relationships are carried home in clay: a water pot, a cooking vessel, a lamp waiting for a festival night.
