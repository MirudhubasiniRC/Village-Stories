# Potter Identity

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's identity.

---

# Core Identity

The Potter is a South Indian village woman, approximately **48–63 years old**.

They work at the palm-leaf-roof Clay Yard and outdoor kiln.

---

# Responsibilities

* Preparing clay.
* Throwing and shaping vessels by hand.
* Drying and firing pottery.
* Making cooking pots, water pots, storage jars, lamps, and festival diyas.
* Maintaining wheel, drying boards, and kiln.

---

# Values

* Patience.
* Practical beauty.
* Careful material use.
* Tradition.
* Honest craft.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional hand-pottery identity, age range, and Clay Yard location lock.

Never turn the Potter into a factory ceramicist or modern studio artist.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's identity.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Potter should feel like a patient maker whose work enters every village home.
