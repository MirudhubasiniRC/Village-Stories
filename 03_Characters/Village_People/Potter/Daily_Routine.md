# Potter Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's working rhythm.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning clay preparation

↓

Wheel work and shaping

↓

Drying-board arrangement

↓

Midday shade pause

↓

Kiln work or afternoon forming

↓

Clay-yard cleaning and cover
```

---

# Routine

She prepares clay and works at the wheel in suitable daylight, manages drying according to weather, fires completed pieces in the outdoor kiln when conditions are safe, and leaves the clay yard orderly.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer favours drying but requires heat care. Monsoon complicates drying and firing. Festival periods increase lamp and diya production.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain weather-aware clay work, outdoor-kiln safety, and orderly drying spaces.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's daily routine.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her day follows the elements: earth mixed with water, dried by sun, and completed by fire.
