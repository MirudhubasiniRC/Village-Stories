# Permanent Identity

Always preserve:

* Female Potter.
* Age approximately 48–63.
* Traditional hand-wheel artisan.
* Palm-leaf-roof Clay Yard and outdoor kiln.

---

# Permanent Appearance and Clothing

Always preserve:

* Warm medium-brown complexion.
* Strong practical medium build.
* Black/early-grey hair in low bun or braid.
* Simple cotton work saree.
* Clay-dusted hands during work.

---

# Permanent Behaviour and Skills

Always preserve:

* Clay preparation, hand-wheel shaping, sun drying, and outdoor-kiln firing.
* Weather awareness.
* Festival diya production.
* Clear child kiln-safety boundaries.

* Turn her into a modern studio artist.
* Use unsafe kiln scenes.
