# Potter Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's practical craft abilities.

---

# Core Skills

* Clay preparation.
* Hand-wheel centring and shaping.
* Making water pots, cooking pots, storage jars, oil lamps, and diyas.
* Sun-drying awareness.
* Outdoor-kiln firing.
* Firing safety.
* Simple pottery repair judgment.

---

# Limitations

She is not:

* An industrial ceramic producer.
* A modern studio-pottery instructor.
* A kiln technician with electric equipment.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional hand wheel, sun drying, and outdoor kiln craft.

Never introduce factory production or electric kilns.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's skills.

---

# Guiding Principle

Her skill is knowing when to touch, when to wait, and when fire is ready.
