# Potter Voice & Body Language

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Potter's voice, body language, and signature sounds.

---

# Voice

Maintain:

* Warm mature female Tamil voice.
* Calm deliberate pace.
* Gentle direct safety guidance.
* Few words while concentrating on the wheel.

---

# Body Language

* Grounded seated posture at wheel.
* Steady wet-clay hand movements.
* Careful carrying of unfired pottery.
* Clear hand signal keeping children away from kiln heat.

---

# Signature Sounds

* Hand wheel turning.
* Wet clay shaped by palms.
* Pot placed on drying board.
* Kiln fire crackle.
* Clay-yard wind beneath palm-leaf roof.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never use exaggerated gestures, modern studio demonstrations, or unsafe kiln action.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Potter's voice and body language.

---

# Guiding Principle

She should be recognizable in sound and motion before she appears: turning wheel, wet clay, quiet instruction.
