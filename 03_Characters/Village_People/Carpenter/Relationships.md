# Carpenter Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's relationships.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is a familiar customer for repairs to outdoor fixtures, supports, or bullock-cart woodwork when appropriate.

Their relationship is practical and respectful.

---

# Relationship With Main Family

Mother may request household shelf or stool repair. Elder Brother can observe only from a safe distance; Younger Daughter remains with an adult and away from sharp tools.

---

# Relationship With Other Artisans

He respects the Blacksmith's metal work and coordinates only when a repair naturally needs both wood and metal.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain fair, practical artisan relationships and child-safety boundaries.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's relationships.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships are built through useful objects returned whole to the homes that need them.
