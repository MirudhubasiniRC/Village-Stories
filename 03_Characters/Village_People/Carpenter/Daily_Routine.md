# Carpenter Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's working rhythm.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Morning workshop setup

↓

Measurements and cutting

↓

Midday shade pause

↓

Afternoon fitting and repairs

↓

Tool cleaning and timber cover
```

---

# Routine

He opens the workshop, checks tools and timber, works in daylight, pauses during harsh heat, completes fittings and repairs in the afternoon, then covers timber and stores hand tools before leaving.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Monsoon requires timber protection. Festival periods can increase demand for stools, shelves, doors, and decorations. Cart repairs may rise around agricultural work.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain daylight hand-tool work and tidy storage.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's daily routine.

---

# Guiding Principle

His day should end with the workshop as orderly as the object he has repaired.
