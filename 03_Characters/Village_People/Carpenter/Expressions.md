# Carpenter Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's facial expressions.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Focused inspection gaze.
* Small satisfied nod when a joint fits.
* Calm customer-listening face.
* Gentle firm safety look toward children.
* Quiet concern over warped or damaged timber.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain understated, patient, work-focused expression.

Never use exaggerated frustration, action-hero intensity, or mocking humour.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's expressions.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should show that careful work is thought before it is action.
