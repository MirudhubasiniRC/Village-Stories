# Carpenter Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's practical craft abilities.

---

# Core Skills

* Measuring timber.
* Hand sawing.
* Planing.
* Chiselling and joinery.
* Door and shelf repair.
* Stool and furniture repair.
* Cart woodwork repair.
* Tool care.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* A modern power-tool workshop owner.
* A civil contractor.
* A blacksmith or mechanic.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional hand-tool craft and durable practical output.

Never introduce factory furniture production or modern machinery as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's skills.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is measured patience—wood cut only after it has been understood.
