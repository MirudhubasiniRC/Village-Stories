# Carpenter Identity

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's identity.

---

# Core Identity

The Carpenter is a South Indian village man, approximately **43–58 years old**, who maintains the village's practical woodwork from an open-sided workshop.

---

# Responsibilities

* Repairing doors, stools, shelves, and furniture.
* Making practical household woodwork.
* Repairing bullock-cart wood parts when appropriate.
* Maintaining workshop tools and timber.

---

# Values

* Careful measurement.
* Durable work.
* Patience.
* Fair service.
* Respect for materials.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain mature traditional artisan identity and Carpenter Workshop location lock.

Never make him a modern furniture designer, factory worker, or unrelated contractor.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's identity.

---

# Guiding Principle

He is the careful hand behind the village's doors, stools, shelves, and cart repairs.
