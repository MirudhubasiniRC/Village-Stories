# Carpenter Continuity

## Purpose

This document acts as the final continuity checklist for the Carpenter.

---

# Permanent Identity

Always preserve:

* Male Carpenter.
* Age approximately 43–58.
* Traditional hand-tool artisan.
* West-side open Carpenter Workshop.

---

# Permanent Appearance and Clothing

Always preserve:

* Warm medium-brown complexion.
* Lean-to-medium practical build.
* Neat moustache.
* Simple cotton shirt and veshti.
* Temporary wood dust during work.

---

# Permanent Behaviour and Skills

Always preserve:

* Measure-before-cut habit.
* Hand saw, plane, chisel, mallet, and joinery work.
* Practical durable repairs.
* Clear child-safety boundaries.

---

# Never List

Never:

* Use modern factory machinery.
* Turn him into a designer-showroom character.
* Use unsafe sharp-tool behaviour.
* Modernize workshop identity.
