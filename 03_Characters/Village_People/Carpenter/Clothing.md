# Carpenter Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's wardrobe.

---

# Everyday Clothing

Maintain:

* Durable simple cotton shirt.
* Practical cotton veshti / dhoti.
* Thundu for sweat and wood dust.
* Bare feet or simple sandals according to work safety.

Use humble colours: cream, dull blue, beige, grey, soft brown, or subtle checks.

---

# Work Adjustments

* Sleeves may be rolled.
* Temporary sawdust on shirt or veshti is natural.
* Clothing remains secure around tools.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never use modern workshop uniforms, designer clothing, jeans, or branded workwear.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's clothing.

---

# Guiding Principle

His clothes should look made for wood dust, shade, and a full day of careful hand work.
