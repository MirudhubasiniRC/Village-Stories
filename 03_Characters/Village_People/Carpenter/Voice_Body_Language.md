# Carpenter Voice & Body Language

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's voice, body language, and signature sounds.

---

# Voice

Maintain:

* Calm medium-low Tamil voice.
* Few practical words while measuring.
* Clear safety guidance.
* Respectful customer conversation.

---

# Body Language

* Grounded workbench stance.
* Measured saw and plane motion.
* Slow inspection of joints.
* Clear signal to keep others away from sharp tools.

---

# Signature Sounds

* Hand saw.
* Wood plane.
* Chisel tap.
* Mallet.
* Timber creak.
* Wood shavings brushed aside.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never use dramatic power-tool noise, loud shouting, or stylized workshop acting.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's voice and body language.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should be recognized by the soft rhythm of woodwork before the camera finds him.
