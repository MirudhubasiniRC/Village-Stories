# Carpenter Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's personality.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Patient.
* Precise.
* Quietly creative.
* Practical.
* Fair.
* Warm after trust is established.

---

# Work Philosophy

He believes a useful object should be sturdy, properly measured, and repaired rather than discarded when possible.

---

# Strengths

* Attention to detail.
* Problem-solving.
* Material awareness.
* Calm customer communication.

---

# Weaknesses

He may become absorbed in a difficult measurement or delay rest during a large repair.

---

# Continuity Rules

Never make him careless, boastful, or a modern design celebrity.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's personality.

---

# Guiding Principle

His character should feel like a well-made stool: quiet, useful, and built to last.
