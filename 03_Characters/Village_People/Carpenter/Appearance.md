# Carpenter Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's appearance.

---

# Appearance

Maintain:

* Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion.
* Approximately 165–174 cm height.
* Lean-to-medium practical build.
* Dark focused eyes.
* Short black hair with natural grey possible.
* Neat moustache, consistently retained once visualized.
* Strong wood-dust-marked hands during work.

He should look patient and capable, not fashionable or cinematic.

---

# Recognizable Features

* Wood-shaving-dusted hands.
* Calm measuring gaze.
* Workshop bench and hand-tool silhouette.
* Simple moustache and practical haircut.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always preserve the same face, build, moustache, and artisan identity.

Never make him a duplicate of Father, Blacksmith, or a modern designer.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Carpenter's appearance.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should look like someone who sees the finished shape inside a rough piece of wood.
