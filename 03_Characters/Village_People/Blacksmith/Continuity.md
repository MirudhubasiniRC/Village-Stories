# Blacksmith Continuity

## Purpose

This document acts as the final continuity checklist for the Blacksmith.

---

# Permanent Identity

Always preserve:

* Male Blacksmith.
* Age approximately 48–60.
* Traditional open-sided forge worker.
* West-side Blacksmith Forge location lock.

---

# Permanent Appearance and Clothing

Always preserve:

* Warm deep medium-brown complexion.
* Strong practical build.
* Neat moustache.
* Simple cotton shirt and veshti.
* Temporary soot/heat details only during work.

---

# Permanent Behaviour and Skills

Always preserve:

* Safe hearth, bellows, anvil, and hand-tool work.
* Honest repair culture.
* Care around children and hot metal.
* Deliberate heat-shape-cool-test rhythm.

---

# Never List

Never:

* Create industrial machinery.
* Turn him into a fantasy weaponsmith.
* Give unsafe child access to forge work.
* Make him an action-hero stereotype.

---

Google Flow must always generate the same Blacksmith at the same tile-roof open forge, with the same face, work clothes, tool language, and controlled craft.
