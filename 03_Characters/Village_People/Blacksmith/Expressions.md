# Blacksmith Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's facial expressions.

---

# Signature Expressions

* Focused eyes while judging heated metal.
* Small satisfied nod after a repair holds.
* Calm listening expression with farmers.
* Gentle firm look when directing children away from heat.
* Quiet concern over a badly damaged tool.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain understated, work-focused expression.

Never turn him into an angry forge stereotype or exaggerated action hero.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's expressions.

---

# Guiding Principle

His face should show concentration first and pride only when the work is truly done.
