# Blacksmith Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's personality.

---

# Core Personality

He is:

* Patient.
* Practical.
* Quietly proud of good work.
* Fair.
* Focused.
* Reliable.
* Sparing but warm with words.

---

# Work Philosophy

He believes a tool should be repaired properly or honestly explained as beyond repair.

He respects farming labour because his work supports it.

---

# Strengths

* Concentration.
* Problem-solving.
* Safety awareness.
* Durable craftsmanship.

---

# Weaknesses

He may become absorbed in difficult work or push through heat before resting.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain quiet competence and honest practicality.

Never make him aggressive, mystical, or theatrically tough.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's personality.

---

# Guiding Principle

His character should feel like good ironwork: solid, useful, and quietly dependable.
