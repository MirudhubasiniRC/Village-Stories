# Blacksmith Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's physical appearance.

---

# Appearance

Maintain:

* Warm deep medium-brown South Indian complexion.
* Approximately 166–174 cm height.
* Strong, practical build from forge work; never exaggerated bodybuilder proportions.
* Dark brown focused eyes.
* Short black hair with natural grey possible.
* Neat moustache.
* Strong scar-free or lightly work-worn hands, unless a future canonical story adds a specific permanent mark.
* Soot, heat sheen, or metal dust only as temporary working detail.

---

# Recognizable Features

* Strong forearms.
* Neat moustache.
* Forge-side silhouette.
* Heat-aware, focused eyes.
* Hammer and anvil context.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always preserve face, build, moustache, and working artisan identity.

Never turn him into a glamour hero, soldier, or unrelated factory worker.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's appearance.

---

# Guiding Principle

He should look like someone shaped by heat, work, and patient craft rather than performance.
