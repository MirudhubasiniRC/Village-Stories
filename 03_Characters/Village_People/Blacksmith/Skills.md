# Blacksmith Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's practical craft abilities.

---

# Core Skills

* Hearth and bellows operation.
* Heating and shaping iron by hand.
* Sharpening sickles, hoes, and blades.
* Small metal repairs.
* Anvil work.
* Cooling and edge testing.
* Tool safety and maintenance.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* An industrial machinist.
* A maker of fantasy weapons.
* A carpenter or mechanic with unrelated mastery.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain traditional forge skill and hand-tool production.

Never introduce welding rigs, powered machinery, or modern factory processes as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Blacksmith's skills.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skill is measured fire: heat applied only where it is needed, then shaped with care.
