# Family Dog Behaviour

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's observable behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

His behaviour should make the compound feel inhabited and safe.

He moves with purpose, rests with familiarity, and interacts with manners.

---

# General Behaviour

He naturally:

- Follows family members at a respectful distance.
- Waits outside the Kitchen.
- Rests in known shade spots.
- Greets Father returning home.
- Plays with Elder Brother.
- Softens around Younger Daughter.
- Visits the Livestock Shelter without agitating animals.
- Avoids damaging Vegetable Garden beds.

---

# Guarding Behaviour

He watches the compound.

Alerts may include:

- Alert ears.
- Standing.
- A few warning barks at unfamiliar night sounds or strangers.

He does not attack as entertainment.
He does not growl constantly.
He is a gentle guardian, not an aggression fantasy.

---

# Livestock Behaviour

Around cows, goats, and chickens:

- Calm.
- Familiar.
- Non-chasing by default.
- Curious watching only.

Any rare chase impulse is corrected by family and not treated as cute cruelty.

---

# Child Behaviour

With Elder Brother:

- Active play partner.
- Trot-and-return games.
- Shared rest after running.

With Younger Daughter:

- Extra gentleness.
- Lowered body language.
- Patient sitting for pets.

---

# Parent Behaviour

With Mother:

- Polite near Kitchen.
- Soft companionship in Backyard and garden edges.

With Father:

- Strong following instinct.
- Greeting ritual when Father returns.
- Outdoor circuit companionship when allowed.

---

# Signature Habits

- Waiting at Kitchen entrance.
- Resting under Front Thinnai or Backyard shade.
- Escorting children within compound.
- Evening perimeter walk feeling.
- Lying with head on paws during family conversation.
- Tail soft-wag greeting.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain peaceful compound manners and family-oriented following.

Never make chicken-chasing or aggression a recurring joke.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Family Dog should be recognized by where he waits and how he watches—loyal orbits around the people he loves.
