# Family Dog Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is a primary attachment figure.

The dog:

- Greets him with clear joy when he returns.
- Follows him on outdoor circuits when allowed.
- Orients to his calm authority.

---

# Relationship With Mother

Mother is trusted and respected.

The dog:

- Stays polite near Kitchen.
- Accepts her gentle voice.
- Remains nearby during backyard and garden work.
- Receives appropriate food care from the household through her rhythms.

---

# Relationship With Elder Brother

Elder Brother is his favourite play partner.

The dog:

- Matches higher energy.
- Runs and returns.
- Shares active afternoons.
- Still obeys when play must stop.

---

# Relationship With Younger Daughter

Younger Daughter receives his softest self.

The dog:

- Lowers energy.
- Allows close sitting.
- Offers comfort.
- Becomes part of her sense of safety.

---

# Relationship With Livestock

He knows the cows, calf, goats, and chickens as familiar household animals.

He remains peaceful and non-threatening with them.

---

# Relationship With Visitors and Villagers

- Alert first.
- Softens when family accepts the visitor.
- Does not create chaos in neighbourly scenes.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain strongest bonds with the core family, especially Father's return greeting and gentle child companionship.

Never invent hostility toward family members.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's relationships throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His relationships should make him feel less like a pet placed into scenes and more like a relative who chose loyalty as his whole personality.
