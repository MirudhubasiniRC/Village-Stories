# Family Dog

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete canonical documentation for the Family Dog, one of the four primary recurring family companions in the Village Stories universe.

He is a German Shepherd and a permanent member of the household's emotional and daily life.

His documentation defines his identity, appearance, personality, behaviour, relationships, routines, skills, and continuity.

Every image prompt, animation, storyboard, and story involving the Family Dog should reference this documentation.

---

# Character Role

The Family Dog is the family's loyal companion and gentle compound guardian.

He watches without aggression, follows without chaos, and comforts without stealing focus from human stories.

He binds outdoor and indoor family life together by simply being present.

---

# Narrative Function

The Family Dog represents:

* Loyalty.
* Home safety.
* Quiet companionship.
* Soft humour.
* Continuity between scenes and spaces.
* Childhood comfort for Elder Brother and Younger Daughter.

---

# Breed Canon

The Family Dog is permanently a:

**German Shepherd**

He must always remain visually and behaviourally a German Shepherd living as a rural Tamil Nadu family dog in the early 1990s.

He is never portrayed as:

* A police dog.
* A military dog.
* An attack dog.
* A show-ring glamorous dog.
* A cartoon mascot.

---
