# Family Dog Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's abilities and learned household competence throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Skills

- Recognizing family members instantly.
- Knowing compound paths and rest spots.
- Waiting outside Kitchen without entering disruptively.
- Gentle play modulation with each child.
- Soft alert barking.
- Following Father on safe routes.
- Remaining calm near livestock.
- Finding shade and water routines.

---

# Social Intelligence

He understands:

- When play is welcome.
- When family is busy.
- When Younger Daughter needs stillness.
- When night requires watching.

---

# Limitations

He is not:

* Formally attack-trained.
* A herding spectacle dog.
* Able to "solve plots" with unnatural intelligence.
* A talking or comedy-humanized character.

His intelligence remains dog-true and impressive within realism.

---

# Signature Skills

- Perfect timing of Father-return greetings.
- Patient Kitchen-door waiting.
- Child-safe play control.
- Compound watchfulness.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always keep skills realistic for a well-loved village German Shepherd.

Never grant superhero or police-dog cinematic abilities as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His skills are the quiet expertise of belonging—knowing where to be, when to wait, and whom to love.
