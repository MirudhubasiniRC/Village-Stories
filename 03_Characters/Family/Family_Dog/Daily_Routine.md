# Family Dog Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's typical daily rhythm throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Dawn stretch / rooster world waking

↓

Morning family greeting

↓

Compound circuits / shade rest

↓

Midday deep rest

↓

Afternoon child play / following work

↓

Evening greeting of Father / family gathering

↓

Night watchfulness and sleep in familiar place
```

---

# Dawn

- Stirs with village morning sounds.
- Stretches.
- Brief trot through Courtyard or Backyard.
- Soft presence near early-rising family members.

---

# Morning

- Greets Mother's outdoor movements.
- May accompany well or garden edges.
- Watches chickens without chasing.
- Rests as heat rises.

---

# Midday

- Deep shade rest.
- Minimal movement in peak heat.
- Occasional water drinking.
- Eyes half-open awareness.

---

# Afternoon

- More activity with Elder Brother after school.
- Gentle company for Younger Daughter.
- Visits Livestock Shelter area calmly.
- Follows outdoor chores at a distance.

---

# Evening

- High social period.
- Greets Father returning.
- Joins Front Thinnai or Courtyard family atmosphere.
- Soft play before night.

---

# Night

- Rests near familiar compound sleeping place.
- Occasional alert to unusual sounds.
- Returns to quiet after recognition of safety.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer: longer shade rest, more panting, less midday travel.

Monsoon: sheltered resting, damp coat, careful wet-yard movement, storm restlessness.

Festivals: more people-watching, higher stimulation, still manners.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain Kitchen waiting, shade resting, Father greeting, and child companionship as recurring daily anchors.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His day should feel like a living clock of the compound—present at the edges of every ordinary hour.
