# Family Dog Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

The Family Dog is:

- Loyal
- Intelligent
- Gentle with family
- Calmly alert
- Patient
- Playful in bursts
- Affectionate
- Steady
- Softly brave

---

# Emotional Nature

He expresses feeling through body and eyes more than noise.

He is emotionally tuned to the household mood.

---

# Temperament

Maintain:

- Stable.
- Warm.
- Watchful without aggression.
- Tolerant with children.
- Peaceful with known livestock.

---

# Strengths

- Reading family routines.
- Waiting patiently.
- Comforting children.
- Alerting to unusual sounds.
- Knowing compound boundaries.

---

# Weaknesses / Limits

He may:

- Become restless before storms.
- Want to follow Father farther than practical.
- React with barking to unfamiliar night sounds.
- Feel anxious if children cry for long.

He never becomes dangerous.

---

# Sense of Play

He enjoys:

- Running with Elder Brother.
- Soft play near Younger Daughter.
- Stick or cloth play when offered.
- Evening stretch-and-trot circuits.

Play remains controlled around chickens and garden beds.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain gentle-guardian loyalty.

Never portray him as vicious, cowardly comic only, or emotionally blank.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His personality should feel like trust—quiet, intelligent, and always oriented toward home.
