# Family Dog Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's facial and body expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

His face and ears tell soft stories.

Emotion is readable without humanized eyebrow acting extremes.

---

# Signature Expressions

- Soft eyes and relaxed ears while resting with family.
- Bright alert ears at unfamiliar sound.
- Warm eye contact during greetings.
- Play-bow invitation with Elder Brother.
- Gentle lowered head near Younger Daughter.
- Focused watching when Father works.
- Content paw-tuck rest at Kitchen entrance.

---

# Common States

Content:

- Soft eyes.
- Loose body.
- Tail easy.

Alert:

- Ears forward.
- Stillness.
- Focused stare.

Joy:

- Active tail.
- Bright movement to greet.

Concern / unease:

- Tense shoulders.
- Brief whine possible.
- Closer proximity to family.

---

# Google Flow Consistency

Same head shape, ear language, eye colour, and emotional subtlety every episode.

Avoid exaggerated anthropomorphic facial animation.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always keep expressions dog-true and family-soft.

Never default to snarl faces.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines the Family Dog's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

His expressions should let viewers know the household mood before any human speaks.
