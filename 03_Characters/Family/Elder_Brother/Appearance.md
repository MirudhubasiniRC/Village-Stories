# Elder Brother Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical physical appearance of Elder Brother throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Identity

Elder Brother is a healthy South Indian village boy living in rural Tamil Nadu during the early 1990s.

His appearance reflects outdoor play, school days, sunlight, and an active childhood near fields and animals.

He should look naturally handsome in a boyish, healthy way—never styled like a child model or cinema child star.

---

# Estimated Age

Approximately:

**9–10 years old**

He must always appear older than Younger Daughter and younger than the parents.

---

# Height

Approximately:

**130–140 cm**

Top of head reaches roughly **Mother's shoulder height** when standing beside her.

Clearly shorter than Father and Mother.

Clearly taller than Younger Daughter.

See `01_Canon/Relative_Height_And_Scale.md`.

---

# Build

Maintain:

- Lean active child build.
- Natural energy.
- Light muscle from play and chores.
- Neither frail nor oversized.

---

# Skin Tone

Maintain:

- Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion.
- Natural sun-kissed tone from outdoor life.
- Healthy skin texture.

---

# Face Shape

Maintain:

- Soft youthful masculine face.
- Gentle suggestion of Father's jaw without adult sharpness.
- Warm open expression.
- Soft cheeks still present with childhood.

He should be recognizable as the son of Father and Mother.

---

# Eyes

Maintain:

- Dark brown.
- Bright.
- Curious.
- Expressive.
- Almond shaped.

---

# Eyebrows

Maintain:

- Natural.
- Softly defined.
- Never shaped.

---

# Nose

Maintain:

- Straight youthful nose.
- Family resemblance to Father in a softened child form.

---

# Lips

Maintain:

- Soft.
- Natural.
- Easy smile.

---

# Hair

Maintain:

- Naturally thick black hair.
- Short to medium rural schoolboy cut.
- Slightly tousled after play.
- Clean for school; more textured after outdoor work.

Never:

- Modern gel styles.
- Fashionable city cuts.
- Overly perfect salon hair.

---

# Recognizable Features

Elder Brother should remain instantly recognizable through:

- Warm medium-brown complexion.
- Thick black schoolboy hair.
- Bright curious dark eyes.
- Active lean build.
- Family resemblance to Father and Mother.
- Open boyish smile.
- Slightly taller stature than Younger Daughter.

---

# Hands and Feet

Hands:

- Small but capable.
- Often dusty after chores.
- Gentle with Younger Daughter and animals.

Feet:

- Barefoot in compound often.
- Simple sandals for school and village paths.

---

# Posture and Movement

Maintain:

- Upright energetic posture.
- Quick but not frantic movement.
- Confident childhood body language.
- Occasional restless shifting when excited.

---

# Environmental Appearance

After play or chores:

- Dust on legs or feet.
- Slightly messy hair.
- Damp hairline after running.

After washing or school preparation:

- Neater hair.
- Clean face and hands.

Temporary changes disappear naturally.

---

# Animation Consistency

Preserve across all Google Flow scenes:

- Face.
- Hair.
- Skin tone.
- Height relative to family.
- Age appearance.
- Body proportions.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- 9–10 year old village boy appearance.
- Warm medium-brown complexion.
- Thick black simple hair.
- Active healthy build.
- Clear older-sibling height above Younger Daughter.

Never:

- Age him into a teenager suddenly.
- Modernize his look.
- Make him look like a different child between episodes.
- Give him adult facial hardness.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's physical appearance throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother should look like a real village boy growing in the sun.

His bright eyes, tousled black hair, healthy medium-brown skin, and active stance should make him instantly familiar in every episode.
