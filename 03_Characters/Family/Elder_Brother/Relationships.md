# Elder Brother Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's personal relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Elder Brother builds relationships through shared play, shared work, loyalty, and everyday closeness.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is Elder Brother's hero of ordinary labour.

Elder Brother:

- Imitates Father.
- Seeks his approval.
- Learns outdoor skills beside him.
- Feels safe in his presence.

Their bond is strong, practical, and affectionate without heavy drama.

---

# Relationship With Mother

Mother is Elder Brother's source of comfort and household guidance.

Elder Brother:

- Softens around her.
- Obeys her more quickly after excitement.
- Relies on her food, care, and calm.
- Shows her school stories and small achievements.

---

# Relationship With Younger Daughter

Younger Daughter is Elder Brother's closest companion and protected sibling.

He:

- Includes her in safe games.
- Walks ahead on unfamiliar paths.
- Comforts her when she is scared.
- Shares snacks and secrets of childhood.
- May boss her briefly, then makes peace.

Their bond should feel warm, lived-in, and enduring.

---

# Relationship With the Family Dog

The German Shepherd is his playmate and guardian companion.

He:

- Runs and rests with the dog.
- Trusts the dog near Younger Daughter.
- Speaks to the dog as a friend.

---

# Relationship With Friends and Village Children

He plays, races, studies, and explores within safe village geography.

Friendships are ordinary and recurring.

---

# Relationship With Teachers and Elders

He shows respect, listens, and represents the family politely in public.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- Deep respect for parents.
- Protective love for Younger Daughter.
- Friendly bond with the Family Dog.
- Warm peer friendships.

Never:

- Create lasting hostility with Younger Daughter.
- Make him disrespectful as a default trait.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's relationships throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother's relationships should make him feel like a real older brother—proud to help, quick to play, and always circling back to family.
