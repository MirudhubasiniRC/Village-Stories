# Elder Brother

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete canonical documentation for the Elder Brother, one of the primary recurring family characters in the Village Stories universe.

He is the older son of Father and Mother, and the protective elder sibling of Younger Daughter.

His documentation defines his identity, appearance, personality, behaviour, relationships, routines, skills, and continuity.

Every image prompt, animation, storyboard, and story involving Elder Brother should reference this documentation.

---

# Character Role

Elder Brother is the family's older child and Father's primary outdoor apprentice.

He learns farming, animal care, and village responsibility through shared daily work.

His curiosity, growing competence, loyalty to Younger Daughter, and respect for his parents shape many childhood stories.

He teaches Younger Daughter gently by example, never by harshness.

---

# Narrative Function

Elder Brother represents:

* Growing responsibility.
* Boyhood curiosity.
* Outdoor learning.
* Sibling protection.
* School and village life.
* Respect for elders.
* Play balanced with duty.

He often bridges the adult work world and childhood play.

---

# Primary Responsibilities

Elder Brother naturally helps with:

* Carrying small tools and baskets.
* Feeding chickens under supervision.
* Assisting Father in garden and shelter tasks.
* Watching Younger Daughter during play.
* Simple school-related routines.
* Errands within familiar village paths.

He is never given adult labour beyond his age.
