# Elder Brother Behaviour

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's observable behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Elder Brother behaves like an active village boy learning by doing.

His actions should feel energetic but guided by respect.

---

# General Behaviour

He naturally behaves in a way that is:

- Active.
- Curious.
- Helpful.
- Respectful to adults.
- Protective toward Younger Daughter.
- Playful with the Family Dog.

---

# Pace

He moves faster than adults.

He slows when:

- Father is teaching carefully.
- Mother asks for calm.
- Animals need gentle handling.
- Younger Daughter needs protection.

---

# Working Behaviour

When helping Father he:

- Watches first.
- Copies carefully.
- Carries what he can safely.
- Asks questions.
- Shows pride when trusted.

He never operates dangerous tools alone.

---

# Sibling Behaviour

Around Younger Daughter he:

- Includes her in play when safe.
- Warns her near animals or tools.
- Sometimes leads; sometimes shares.
- Comforts her if she is upset.
- May show brief childish impatience, then softens.

---

# School Behaviour

At school and on the school path he:

- Walks with familiar village children.
- Greets teachers respectfully.
- Carries simple school materials.
- Returns home with stories and hunger.

---

# Behaviour Around Parents

With Father:

- Eager to help.
- Listens closely during outdoor work.
- Seeks quiet approval.

With Mother:

- Softens indoors.
- Obeys household guidance.
- Enjoys her food and care.
- Becomes gentler in the Kitchen doorway and Courtyard.

---

# Behaviour Around the Family Dog

He:

- Plays fetch-like running games carefully.
- Rests against the German Shepherd after play.
- Speaks excitedly but kindly.
- Never harms or teases cruelly.

---

# Signature Habits

- Running into the Courtyard after school.
- Squatting beside Father during repairs.
- Calling Younger Daughter before going out to play.
- Scratching the Family Dog behind the ears.
- Dusting hands on shorts after chores.
- Standing taller when given responsibility.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- Helpful outdoor behaviour.
- Protective sibling actions.
- Respectful responses to parents.
- Age-appropriate energy.

Never:

- Make him reckless without consequence and correction.
- Show cruelty.
- Have him supervise as an adult would.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother should be recognized by how he moves through the compound—half play, half duty, always circling back to family.
