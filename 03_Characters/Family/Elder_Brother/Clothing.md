# Elder Brother Clothing

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Elder Brother dresses with simple rural practicality.

Clothes support school, play, and light chores—not fashion.

Every garment should feel authentic to early-1990s rural Tamil Nadu.

---

# Everyday Clothing

Canonical everyday clothing:

- Simple cotton shirt or banian/vest with shirt depending on activity.
- Short cotton trousers or folded practical bottoms suitable for a village boy.
- Occasional simple shorts for heavy play or hot days at home.
- Bare feet in compound; sandals outside.

Default look should feel neat enough for home and chores, not formal.

---

# School Clothing

For school scenes:

- Clean simple shirt.
- Neat shorts or school-appropriate trousers common to rural Tamil Nadu in the early 1990s.
- Hair combed.
- Sandals.

No modern branded uniforms or logos unless a future documented school canon requires a specific local style. Keep it humble and period-correct.

---

# Colour Palette

Preferred:

- White
- Cream
- Light blue
- Soft brown
- Faded green
- Simple checks

Avoid neon colours and modern prints.

---

# Festival Clothing

- Cleaner shirt.
- Neater trousers or veshti-style ceremonial dressing only when culturally appropriate for a boy at festivals.
- Freshly washed appearance.
- Still modest and childlike.

---

# Work and Play Adjustments

During chores:

- Shirt may become slightly dusty.
- Sleeves may be rolled if present.
- Feet often bare in compound.

During play:

- Clothes may wrinkle.
- Minor grass or dust marks are natural.

---

# Footwear

- Barefoot at home and compound when practical.
- Simple rubber or leather sandals for school and village.

No sports sneakers as a signature look.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- Simple cotton rural boy clothing.
- Period-correct early 1990s look.
- Practical play-ready clothes.
- Cleaner appearance for school and festivals.

Never:

- Designer child fashion.
- Modern graphic tees.
- Branded sportswear as default.
- Oversized cinematic styling.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's wardrobe throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother's clothes should look lived in by a real village schoolboy—clean for learning, ready for play, and honest after chores.
