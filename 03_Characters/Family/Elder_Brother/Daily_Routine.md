# Elder Brother Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's typical daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

His day follows school, family labour rhythms, play, and rest.

Season and school calendar may adjust timing, but the pattern remains familiar.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Wake

↓

Wash / Morning readiness

↓

Morning tea / breakfast

↓

School or morning help (season / holiday)

↓

Return home

↓

Lunch

↓

Afternoon play / light chores / study

↓

Evening help with Father or animals

↓

Family time / dinner

↓

Rest
```

---

# Morning

Elder Brother usually:

- Wakes after parents have begun the day.
- Washes at the Family Well or designated area.
- Eats breakfast prepared by Mother.
- Prepares for school on school days.
- On holidays, joins Father for light outdoor help.

---

# School Days

He:

- Leaves along familiar village paths.
- Attends Primary School.
- Plays briefly with friends when appropriate.
- Returns home by familiar routes.

---

# After School / Afternoon

He often:

- Changes into more play-ready clothes if needed.
- Eats leftover or fresh food as offered.
- Plays in Courtyard, Backyard, or Playground.
- Includes Younger Daughter when she wants to join.
- Completes simple homework or reading when required.

---

# Evening

He often:

- Helps Father with light Livestock Shelter or garden tasks.
- Feeds chickens with Younger Daughter under supervision.
- Plays with the Family Dog.
- Washes before dinner.
- Shares evening family time.

---

# Night

- Dinner with family.
- Quiet winding down.
- Sleep after a full child day.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer:

- More early outdoor play.
- Reduced harsh midday running.

Monsoon:

- Indoor-adjacent play.
- Careful wet-path walking.
- Paper boats and rain joy when safe.

Harvest / holidays:

- More time helping Father.
- Greater tiredness and pride.

Festival days:

- Clean clothes.
- Family and temple participation.
- Extra excitement.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- School rhythm on school days.
- Help balanced with play.
- Evening family return.
- Age-appropriate chores only.

Never:

- Keep him working like an adult all day.
- Remove childhood play from his life.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother's day should feel like a real village childhood—school dust, courtyard play, evening chores, and the comfort of coming home.
