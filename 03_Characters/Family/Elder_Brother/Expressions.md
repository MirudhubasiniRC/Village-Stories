# Elder Brother Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's facial expressions and emotional presentation throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Elder Brother's face is open and readable.

His emotions are honest and age-appropriate, never overacted into cartoon extremes.

---

# Neutral Expression

- Soft alert face.
- Bright resting eyes.
- Relaxed mouth.

---

# Signature Expressions

- Eager smile when helping Father.
- Proud grin after completing a task.
- Gentle look toward Younger Daughter.
- Excited eyes before play.
- Focused brows while learning.
- Softened face with Mother.
- Quick concern if Younger Daughter cries.

---

# Common Emotions

Happy:

- Wide natural smile.
- Bright eyes.

Concentration:

- Slight lip press.
- Focused gaze.

Concern:

- Soft furrowed brows.
- Forward lean.

Mischief:

- Small sideways smile.
- Never cruel.

Tiredness:

- Quieter eyes.
- Slower blinks after long play or chores.

---

# Google Flow Consistency

Maintain the same face, smile style, and emotional subtlety across all scenes.

Avoid exaggerated cartoon acting.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain open, warm, boyish expressiveness.

Never make him emotionally blank or adult-hardened.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother's face should tell childhood clearly—eagerness, pride, care, and play—without needing dialogue.
