# Elder Brother Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's developing knowledge and abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Elder Brother is learning.

His skills are real for his age, never adult-level mastery.

---

# Core Developing Skills

- Age-appropriate farming help.
- Chicken feeding and egg-gathering support.
- Carrying light loads.
- Basic animal awareness.
- Simple repairs observation.
- School learning.
- Village path familiarity.
- Sibling care judgment.

---

# Outdoor Skills

He can:

- Carry small baskets and tools.
- Help Father place supports.
- Sweep shelter areas lightly.
- Recognize common animals' moods at a basic level.
- Follow irrigation and garden instructions under supervision.

He cannot:

- Handle heavy adult loads alone.
- Use dangerous tools unsupervised.
- Lead bullock cart independently.

---

# Household Skills

He can:

- Wash hands and feet properly.
- Help bring washed items.
- Run simple errands inside the compound.
- Sit respectfully during prayer moments.

Mother remains the household authority.

---

# Social Skills

- Greeting elders.
- Playing fairly.
- Sharing with Younger Daughter.
- Listening to teachers.

---

# Signature Skills

Elder Brother is especially recognized for:

- Being Father's eager helper.
- Knowing the compound paths by heart.
- Gentle leadership with Younger Daughter.
- Energetic play with the Family Dog.
- Quick learning through imitation.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- Developing competence.
- Clear age limits.
- Learning through supervision.

Never:

- Make him equal to Father in skill.
- Give him modern technical abilities.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Elder Brother's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Elder Brother's skills should feel like seeds of Father's knowledge—growing honestly, watered by repetition, never forced into premature mastery.
