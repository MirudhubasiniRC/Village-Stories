# Mother

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete canonical documentation for the Mother, one of the four primary recurring characters in the Village Stories universe.

She is the emotional centre of the family and appears in the majority of stories.

Her documentation defines her identity, appearance, personality, behaviour, relationships, routines, skills, and continuity.

Every image prompt, animation, storyboard, and story involving Mother should reference this documentation.

---

# Character Role

Mother is the primary caretaker of the household.

She preserves traditional knowledge through everyday life.

Her cooking, gardening, household management, compassion, patience, and quiet strength shape the emotional atmosphere of the family.

She teaches not through formal instruction but through daily example.

---

# Narrative Function

Mother represents:

* Care.
* Patience.
* Wisdom.
* Hospitality.
* Traditional cooking.
* Family unity.
* Practical knowledge.
* Emotional stability.

She is rarely the loudest person in a scene, yet she is often its emotional focus.

---

# Primary Responsibilities

Mother naturally manages:

* Traditional cooking.
* Kitchen organization.
* Vegetable preparation.
* Garden harvesting.
* Household management.
* Food preservation.
* Caring for family members.
* Teaching Daughter traditional skills.

She performs these responsibilities calmly and confidently.

---

# Relationships

Mother shares important relationships with:

* Father.
* Daughter.
* Family Dog.
* Neighbours.
* Village elders.
* Village children.

Individual relationship details are documented separately.

---

# Typical Locations

Mother most frequently appears in:

* Kitchen.
* Courtyard.
* Backyard.
* Vegetable Garden.
* Front Garden.
* Water Well.
* Veranda.

She occasionally visits other village locations when appropriate.

# Story Themes

Stories involving Mother commonly explore:
