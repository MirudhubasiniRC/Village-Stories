# Mother Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical physical appearance of Mother throughout the Village Stories universe.

It establishes her facial features, body proportions, hairstyle, complexion, posture, movement, and recognizable visual characteristics.

This document defines only her physical appearance.

Clothing, personality, behaviour, and expressions are documented separately.

---

# Permanent Jewellery Identity

Mother's recurring jewellery identity is deliberately modest and stable:

* Small traditional gold jhumkas.
* One simple gold chain carrying her traditional thali pendant.
* Small gold nose pin on the left nostril.
* Thin glass bangles.

Her jewellery communicates married village life and personal care; it never becomes ornate, heavy, or fashion-led.

No additional necklaces should appear unless a specific festival or ceremony is documented.

---

# Identity

Mother is a healthy South Indian village woman living in rural Tamil Nadu during the 1990s.

Her appearance reflects a lifetime of outdoor living, traditional cooking, gardening, and caring for her family.

She should appear naturally beautiful through authenticity rather than glamour.

# Hairstyle

Mother's permanent everyday hairstyle is a traditional South Indian half-up hairstyle.

Maintain:

- Upper section gently gathered and secured at the back.
- Remaining hair flowing naturally down the back.
- Soft waves through the lower half.
- Clean, neat, timeless appearance.

No modern salon styling.

This hairstyle is canonical.

Cooking or heavy work:

- Hair may temporarily be tied into a low bun or simple braid for safety.

After work:

- Hair returns to the canonical half-up hairstyle.

Festivals:

- Fresh jasmine flowers may be added.

Weddings and major ceremonies:

- Traditional Jadai Alangaram may be worn.

These ceremonial ornaments are never used during ordinary days.

# Bangles

Their gentle glass clinking becomes one of Mother's recognizable audio signatures throughout the series.

Avoid heavy metal bangles for everyday wear.

# Guiding Principle

Mother's appearance should reflect a lifetime of quiet care.

Her warm smile, traditional half-up hairstyle, small red tilak bindi, gold nose pin, gently moving small jhumkas, one simple gold thali chain, softly chiming glass bangles, skilled hands, and calm presence should make her instantly recognizable, allowing viewers to feel they are meeting the same familiar person in every story.
