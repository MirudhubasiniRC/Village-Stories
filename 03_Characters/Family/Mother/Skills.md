# Mother Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Mother's knowledge, practical abilities, traditional skills, and life experience throughout the Village Stories universe.

These skills have been developed through years of observation, practice, family tradition, and everyday village life.

This document defines what Mother knows and what she is capable of doing.

---

# Philosophy

Mother is highly skilled, but never boastful.

Her abilities are the result of years of experience rather than formal education.

She performs most tasks naturally without drawing attention to her expertise.

---
