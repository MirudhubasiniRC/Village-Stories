# Younger Daughter Daily Routine

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's typical daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Daily Timeline

```text
Wake

↓

Wash / readiness

↓

Breakfast with family

↓

Morning with Mother / play / light learning

↓

Lunch

↓

Afternoon rest or soft play

↓

Evening family time / light helping

↓

Dinner

↓

Rest
```

---

# Morning

Younger Daughter usually:

- Wakes after Mother's earliest work has begun.
- Washes with help as needed.
- Eats breakfast.
- Stays near Mother during morning household rhythm.
- Plays in Courtyard or Front Thinnai when free.

If school-readiness or early school applies in a story, keep it gentle and age-true; she remains younger than Elder Brother's full school independence.

---

# Midday

- Lunch with family.
- Possible short rest in heat.
- Quiet indoor play during heavy sun or rain.

---

# Afternoon

- Soft play with Elder Brother when he returns.
- Garden visits with Mother.
- Sitting with the Family Dog.
- Tiny helping tasks.

---

# Evening

- Family gathering energy.
- Light chicken feeding help under supervision.
- Watching Father return.
- Dinner closeness.
- Night prayer participation in a childlike way.
- Sleep.

---

# Seasonal Variations

Summer: more shade play, early mornings outdoors.

Monsoon: indoor wonder, rain watching from shelter, careful wet feet.

Festivals: excitement, special clothes, staying close in crowds.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain Mother-centered mornings, sibling afternoons, and gentle evening family return.

Never schedule her like an adult worker.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's daily routine throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter's day should feel safe, warm, and full of small discoveries between breakfast and sleep.
