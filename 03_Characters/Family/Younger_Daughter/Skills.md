# Younger Daughter Skills

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's developing abilities throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Younger Daughter is at the beginning of learning.

Her skills are small, sincere, and supervised.

---

# Core Developing Skills

- Watching and remembering steps.
- Tiny vegetable washing help.
- Carrying very light items.
- Gathering flowers or herbs with Mother.
- Gentle animal greeting.
- Simple tidying of play things.
- Basic prayer posture imitation.
- Early counting, songs, and school-readiness learning when relevant.

---

# Kitchen Learning

She may:

- Hand Mother curry leaves.
- Place washed items where shown.
- Observe grinding and cooking from a safe distance.

She may not:

- Manage fire.
- Use sharp tools alone.
- Lift heavy pots.

---

# Garden Learning

She may:

- Point out ripe vegetables Mother confirms.
- Water lightly with a small vessel under guidance.
- Collect fallen flowers.

---

# Social Skills

- Greeting elders softly.
- Sharing with Elder Brother.
- Saying thanks.
- Comforting through closeness.

---

# Signature Skills

- Excellent watching.
- Soft animal manners.
- Quick emotional reading of family faces.
- Remembering small household rituals.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always keep skills age-limited and supervised.

Never portray her as independently running household work.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's skills throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter's skills should feel like beginnings—tiny, careful, and full of love.
