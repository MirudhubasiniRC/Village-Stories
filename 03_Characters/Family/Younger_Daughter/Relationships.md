# Younger Daughter Relationships

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's personal relationships within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Relationship With Mother

Mother is Younger Daughter's closest daily teacher and comfort.

Younger Daughter:

- Follows Mother through Kitchen and garden rhythms.
- Learns by watching.
- Seeks her for reassurance.
- Feels safest near her.

This is one of the emotional centres of the series.

---

# Relationship With Father

Father is admired and gently loved.

Younger Daughter:

- Watches his outdoor strength with wonder.
- Accepts short safe tasks proudly.
- Softens into shyness-smiles around him.
- Feels protected when he is home.

---

# Relationship With Elder Brother

Elder Brother is her hero of play and protection.

Younger Daughter:

- Follows him.
- Trusts his lead on safe adventures.
- Shares secrets of childhood.
- Calls him when scared.
- Learns social courage through him.

Their bond is foundational.

---

# Relationship With the Family Dog

The German Shepherd is her living comfort.

She:

- Sits against him.
- Speaks softly.
- Feels braver with him nearby.
- Shares quiet companionship in Backyard shade.

---

# Relationship With Village Children and Elders

- Soft friendliness with known children.
- Shyness that warms with familiarity.
- Respectful greeting of elders.
- Happy acceptance of neighbour kindness.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain strongest bonds with Mother, Elder Brother, Father, and Family Dog.

Never create lasting alienation from family.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's relationships throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter's relationships should feel like soft threads tying the whole family together.
