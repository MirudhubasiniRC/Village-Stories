# Younger Daughter Behaviour

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's observable behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# General Behaviour

She naturally behaves in a way that is:

- Gentle.
- Curious.
- Close to family.
- Eager to imitate Mother.
- Trusting of Elder Brother.
- Affectionate with the Family Dog.

---

# Pace

She moves lightly.

She pauses often to watch details—insects, flour, flowers, chicken movement, rain.

---

# Helping Behaviour

When helping Mother she:

- Stands close.
- Holds small vessels carefully.
- Watches before touching.
- Smiles when praised.
- Accepts gentle correction.

She never cooks on the clay stove alone.

---

# Sibling Behaviour

Around Elder Brother she:

- Follows on safe adventures.
- Holds his hand when paths feel busy.
- Shares toys and snacks.
- Calls for him when unsure.
- Plays house, courtyard games, and soft pretend.

---

# Behaviour Around Parents

With Mother:

- Stays near Kitchen and garden learning spaces.
- Touches saree edge for comfort.
- Shows her found flowers or feathers.

With Father:

- Becomes a little shy-proud.
- Watches his work with awe.
- Accepts short safe tasks happily.

---

# Behaviour Around the Family Dog

She:

- Sits beside the German Shepherd.
- Pets gently under supervision habits.
- Talks softly to him.
- Feels protected in his presence.

---

# Signature Habits

- Peeping into the Kitchen from the doorway.
- Carrying a tiny basket imitation.
- Holding Elder Brother's fingers.
- Resting against the Family Dog.
- Showing Mother a leaf or flower.
- Quiet dancing steps in courtyard dust.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain soft helpful behaviour and sibling closeness.

Never place her in unsafe unsupervised labour.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's behaviour throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter should be recognized by how she stays close—watching, helping in miniature, and turning ordinary corners of the home into wonder.
