# Younger Daughter Identity

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical identity of Younger Daughter within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Identity

Younger Daughter is one of the primary recurring family characters of Village Stories.

She is the younger child of Father and Mother and the little sister of Elder Brother.

Her identity is built upon gentleness, curiosity, affection, imagination, and eager learning.

She wants to help.

She wants to understand.

She wants to stay close to the people she loves.

---

# Narrative Role

Younger Daughter represents:

* Innocence.
* Wonder.
* Soft courage.
* Traditional learning beginning.
* Emotional warmth of childhood.

---

# Family Role

Within the family she:

* Learns household life from Mother.
* Receives outdoor protection and short lessons from Father.
* Follows and trusts Elder Brother.
* Treats the Family Dog as a beloved companion.

She is never burdened with adult responsibility.

---

# Personal Values

* Kindness.
* Sharing.
* Curiosity.
* Closeness to family.
* Gentleness with animals.
* Trying her best.

---

# Strengths

* Observant.
* Affectionate.
* Quick to learn small tasks.
* Emotionally sincere.
* Brave in tiny steps when supported.

---

# Limitations

She may:

* Become shy with strangers.
* Tire quickly.
* Fear loud thunder or unfamiliar animals at first.
* Need reassurance.
* Spill or drop things while learning.

These remain tender and believable.

---

# Motivations

* To help Mother.
* To keep up with Elder Brother when safe.
* To care for the Family Dog.
* To learn grown-up looking tasks in miniature.
* To feel included.

---

# Story Function

She often anchors stories of:

* First learning.
* Soft emotional moments.
* Discovery in garden and kitchen.
* Sibling bonds.
* Festival awe.

---

# Character Arc

She grows gradually through confidence, vocabulary, skills, and courage.

Her core gentle identity remains constant.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

* Younger-sibling softness.
* Strong bond with Mother.
* Trust in Elder Brother.
* Childlike wonder.

Never:

* Make her harsh.
* Age her into Elder Brother's peer suddenly.
* Force adult emotional complexity as default.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's identity within the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter is love learning how to live.

Her greatest strength is the open-hearted way she watches, helps, and belongs.
