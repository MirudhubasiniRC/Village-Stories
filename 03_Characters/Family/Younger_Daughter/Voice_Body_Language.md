# Younger Daughter Voice & Body Language

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's voice, speech, body language, and signature sounds throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Voice Characteristics

- Soft child voice.
- Higher pitch appropriate to age 6–7.
- Clear but gentle.
- Becomes tinier when shy.
- Brightens when excited.

---

# Speaking Style

- Short sentences.
- Many questions.
- Soft calls for Mother or Elder Brother.
- Gentle talk to the Family Dog.
- Polite greetings to elders.

---

# Movement

- Light footsteps.
- Occasional skipping.
- Hand-holding with Elder Brother.
- Staying near Mother's side.
- Careful small helping motions.

---

# Signature Gestures

- Holding Mother's saree edge.
- Offering a flower with both hands.
- Petting the German Shepherd gently.
- Covering ears in thunder.
- Clapping softly when happy.

---

# Signature Sounds

- Soft footsteps in courtyard dust.
- Small laughter.
- Calling Elder Brother.
- Quiet Kitchen doorway watching.
- Gentle dog talk.

---

# Google Flow Performance Rules

Natural child motion, soft voice consistency, no overacting.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain soft voice and light movement.

Never age her voice upward into Elder Brother's range.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's voice and body language throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter should be recognizable by her soft voice, light steps, and the way she stays close to love.
