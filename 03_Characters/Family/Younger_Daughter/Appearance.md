# Younger Daughter Appearance

## Purpose

This document permanently defines the canonical physical appearance of Younger Daughter throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Identity

Younger Daughter is a healthy South Indian village girl living in rural Tamil Nadu during the early 1990s.

She should appear naturally lovely through innocence and health—never through child glamour, makeup, or modern styling.

---

# Estimated Age

Approximately:

**6–7 years old**

Always clearly younger and smaller than Elder Brother.

---

# Height

Approximately:

**115–125 cm**

Shorter than Elder Brother.

Much shorter than Father and Mother.

---

# Build

Maintain:

- Small healthy child build.
- Soft proportions.
- Natural energy without wiry hardness.

---

# Skin Tone

Maintain:

- Warm medium-brown South Indian complexion matching the family.
- Soft natural texture.
- Mild outdoor sun kiss.

---

# Face Shape

Maintain:

- Soft round-oval child face.
- Gentle resemblance to Mother.
- Warm cheeks.
- Open innocent expression.

---

# Eyes

Maintain:

- Dark brown.
- Large and expressive for her face.
- Curious.
- Soft.

---

# Eyebrows

- Soft natural child brows.

---

# Nose

- Small soft nose with gentle family resemblance to Mother.

---

# Lips

- Soft.
- Natural.
- Easy gentle smile.

---

# Hair

Maintain:

- Naturally black.
- Thick.
- Long enough for simple rural girl styling.
- Usually tied in two braids, one braid, or neat simple tying for play and home.
- Small ribbon or cotton band acceptable.
- Fresh flowers only on festivals or special days.

Never:

- Modern salon cuts.
- Overly ornate daily hair.
- Makeup of any kind.

---

# Forehead

- Clean child forehead.
- No everyday adult bindi required.
- Tiny decorative bindi only for festivals if culturally appropriate and story-justified.

---

# Recognizable Features

- Warm medium-brown complexion.
- Soft Mother-like facial warmth.
- Dark expressive eyes.
- Neat black braids or simple tied hair.
- Smaller stature than Elder Brother.
- Gentle smile.
- Small helpful hands.

---

# Hands and Feet

Hands:

- Small.
- Often lightly flour-dusted or garden-touched after helping.
- Careful when guided.

Feet:

- Often barefoot in compound.
- Simple sandals outside.

---

# Posture and Movement

- Light steps.
- Occasional skipping.
- Closeness to Mother's saree edge or Elder Brother's side.
- Curious pausing to watch details.

---

# Animation Consistency

Preserve face, hair style family, skin tone, height relationships, and age appearance in every Google Flow scene.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain 6–7 year old village girl appearance and clear younger-sibling scale.

Never modernize, glamorize, or age her suddenly.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's physical appearance throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter should look like the familiar little girl of the house—soft-eyed, braid-neat, sun-warmed, and easy to recognize beside Mother and Elder Brother.
