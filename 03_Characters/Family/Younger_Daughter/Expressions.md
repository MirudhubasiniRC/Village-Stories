# Younger Daughter Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's facial expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Philosophy

Her face is highly readable and tender.

Emotions are honest, never exaggerated into cartoon extremes — **except** the
Sudden Rain Loud Face lock (CANON-2026-006), which must read big on a phone.

---

# Signature Expressions

- Wide curious eyes while watching Mother cook.
- Soft closed-mouth smile when praised.
- Bright open smile during play.
- Concerned small frown when unsure.
- Tearful eyes that recover quickly with comfort.
- Quiet awe during gentle rain that is already falling.
- **Sudden rain burst (CANON-2026-006):** loud delighted/startled face — eyes
  huge, mouth open shout or gasp, whole face commits; may squeal-laugh then run
  for cover. Never a calm quiet face on sudden onset.
- Sleepy softness at night.

---

# Common Emotions

Joy: bright eyes, quick smile.

Curiosity: forward lean, parted lips.

Affection: warm gaze toward family or dog.

Fear: widened eyes, closeness to Mother or Elder Brother.

Pride: shy smile after tiny success.

---

# Google Flow Consistency

Same face, eye shape, smile style, and emotional subtlety every time.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always keep expressions gentle and age-true.

Never harden her face into adult sarcasm or blankness.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's expressions throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter's face should make viewers feel protective tenderness immediately.
