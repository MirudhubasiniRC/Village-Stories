# Younger Daughter Personality

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Core Personality

Younger Daughter is:

- Gentle
- Curious
- Affectionate
- Observant
- Playful in soft ways
- Eager to help
- Occasionally shy
- Brave in small steps
- Deeply attached to family

---

# Emotional Nature

She feels vividly and shows emotion honestly.

Comfort returns quickly through Mother, Elder Brother, or the Family Dog.

---

# Temperament

- Warm.
- Soft-spoken.
- Easily delighted.
- Sensitive to scolding tone.
- Quick to hug or stay close.

---

# Strengths

- Empathy.
- Careful watching.
- Sweet persistence in learning tiny tasks.
- Kindness to animals.

---

# Weaknesses

- May cling when uncertain.
- May become tearful when overwhelmed.
- May underestimate danger without guidance.
- May get distracted mid-task.

---

# Sense of Humor

She laughs in bright small bursts.

She enjoys gentle play, funny animal moments, and Elder Brother's safe teasing.

---

# Fears

- Loud thunder.
- Being left behind.
- Sharp tools.
- Unfamiliar large animals until reassured.

---

# Hopes

- To help Mother like a grown daughter.
- To be included by Elder Brother.
- To make family smile.
- To care for the Family Dog.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain gentleness, curiosity, and family attachment.

Never make her mean, highly sarcastic, or emotionally hardened.

---

# Canon Authority

This document permanently defines Younger Daughter's personality throughout the Village Stories universe.

---

# Guiding Principle

Younger Daughter's personality should feel like quiet sunlight in the house—soft, curious, and deeply loving.
