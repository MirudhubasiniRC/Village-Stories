# Father Expressions

## Purpose

This document permanently defines Father's facial expressions, eye expressions, smiles, emotional reactions, and subtle mannerisms throughout the Village Stories universe.

These expressions should remain consistent across every image prompt, Google Flow scene, storyboard, animation, and illustration.

This document defines **how Father expresses emotion**, not **what he feels internally**.

---

# Philosophy

Father rarely displays exaggerated emotions.

His face is naturally calm, masculine, and quietly expressive.

Even strong emotions are communicated through subtle changes in his eyes, mouth, brow, posture, and small pauses.

The audience should recognize Father's feelings immediately without dramatic acting.

---

# Expression Principles

Father's expressions should always feel:

- Genuine.
- Grounded.
- Warm.
- Calm.
- Natural.
- Understated.

Avoid exaggerated acting or cinematic hero intensity.

---

# Neutral Expression

Father's resting expression is steady and peaceful.

Maintain:

- Relaxed eyebrows.
- Observant eyes.
- Softly set mouth.
- Subtle moustache framing a calm face.
- No default hardness or anger.

He should never appear threatening or blank by default.

---

# Gentle Smile

This is one of Father's signature expressions.

Maintain:

- Closed-mouth or softly parted smile.
- Slight upward curve of the lips.
- Warm eyes.
- Softening around the jaw.

This smile appears while:

- Returning home.
- Watching the children learn.
- Greeting Mother.
- Seeing healthy crops.
- Resting on the Front Thinnai.
- Looking at the Family Dog.

This should be among Father's most frequent expressions.

---

# Happy Smile

When genuinely happy:

Maintain:

- Brighter eyes.
- Wider but natural smile.
- Relaxed face.
- Soft laugh readiness.

Never exaggerate into glamorous or cartoonish grinning.

---

# Laugh

Father laughs softly to moderately, never theatrically.

Maintain:

- Warm smile.
- Slight shoulder ease.
- Natural eye narrowing.
- Genuine sound implied by face and breath.

Laughter should feel earned by the moment.

---

# Concentration

While farming, repairing, or reading weather:

Maintain:

- Slightly narrowed eyes.
- Soft forehead focus.
- Closed or lightly pressed lips.
- Steady gaze on the task.

His concentration remains calm and practical.

---

# Teaching Expression

While teaching Elder Brother or Younger Daughter:

Maintain:

- Encouraging small smile.
- Patient eyes.
- Attentive stillness.
- Calm confidence.

Learning should always feel safe.

---

# Caring Expression

When caring for family or animals:

Maintain:

- Softened eyes.
- Relaxed brows.
- Gentle mouth.
- Quiet attention.

This expression should communicate reassurance and responsibility.

---

# Pride

Father's pride is quiet.

Maintain:

- Gentle smile.
- Brightened eyes.
- Slight nod.
- Calm satisfaction.

Examples:

- Elder Brother or Younger Daughter completes a small outdoor task.
- A successful harvest stage.
- Animals healthy after a difficult day.
- A repair that holds.

---

# Curiosity

Maintain:

- Slight eyebrow lift.
- Attentive eyes.
- Still mouth.
- Head tip only if natural.

Curiosity should appear thoughtful rather than startled.

---

# Surprise

Father rarely shows dramatic surprise.

Maintain:

- Slightly widened eyes.
- Small eyebrow lift.
- Brief pause.

Avoid exaggerated reactions.

---

# Concern

When worried:

Maintain:

- Soft furrow between eyebrows.
- Focused eyes.
- Firmer mouth.
- Reduced smile.

Concern should communicate care and calculation rather than panic.

Common sources:

- Delayed rain.
- Animal illness signs.
- Damaged fence or tools.
- Family fatigue or sickness.

---

# Sadness

Sadness remains quiet.

Maintain:

- Lowered gaze.
- Softened features.
- Calm breathing.
- Minimal tears unless the story genuinely requires them.

Father rarely loses emotional control.

---

# Relief

After difficulties pass:

Maintain:

- Gentle exhale.
- Soft smile.
- Relaxed shoulders.
- Warmer eyes.

Relief often follows rain arrival, safe return, completed repairs, or recovered animals.

---

# Gratitude

Maintain:

- Small smile.
- Respectful nod.
- Warm eye contact.

Gratitude is often shown through continued helpful action as much as expression.

---

# Affection

Father expresses affection through:

- Gentle smiles.
- Soft eye contact.
- Brief caring touch when appropriate.
- Protective attention.
- Quiet presence beside Mother and the children.

Physical affection remains natural, modest, and culturally grounded.

---

# Eye Expressions

Father's eyes often communicate emotion before his mouth does.

Maintain:

- Steady eye movement.
- Warm eye contact with family.
- Practical scanning of land and animals.
- Patient observation.

Avoid rapid or exaggerated eye movements.

---

# Brows

Eyebrows move subtly.

Maintain:

- Gentle focus lines during work.
- Soft lifts for curiosity.
- No exaggerated frowning as a default look.

---

# Mouth and Moustache

Maintain:

- Relaxed mouth at rest.
- Natural smiles that work with the subtle moustache.
- Soft speaking posture.
- No glamorous posed mouth shapes.

---

# Expressions While Working

Common expressions include:

- Quiet concentration.
- Practical satisfaction after a finished stage.
- Brief skyward weather checks.
- Soft smile when work goes well.
- Focused seriousness when tools or animals need care.

Work faces should feel lived-in, not performed.

---

# Expressions Around Nature

Father often softens while:

- Watching first rain.
- Seeing healthy green growth.
- Feeling evening breeze after labour.
- Listening to birds near fields.
- Observing canal water.

Nature brings visible peace and sometimes quiet hope.

---

# Expressions Around the Children

Maintain:

- Encouragement.
- Patience.
- Pride.
- Protective attention.
- Gentle amusement.

His expressions help Elder Brother and Younger Daughter feel capable and safe.

---

# Expressions Around Mother

Maintain:

- Quiet understanding.
- Warm smiles.
- Mutual respect.
- Softened tiredness after returning home.
- Shared satisfaction.

Their affection is communicated subtly.

---

# Expressions Around the Family Dog

Maintain:

- Soft smile.
- Familiar eyes.
- Mild amusement.
- Easy recognition.

The dog is treated as family.

---

# Signature Expressions

Father is most recognizable through:

- Calm resting face with subtle moustache.
- Gentle closed-mouth smile when returning home.
- Focused outdoor concentration.
- Soft pride while watching the children.
- Warm quiet look toward Mother.
- Concerned skyward weather glance.
- Peaceful Front Thinnai rest expression.

These expressions should become familiar to viewers.

---

# Google Flow Expression Consistency

Across all Google Flow scenes:

Maintain:

- The same facial proportions.
- The same smile style.
- The same eye shape.
- The same subtle moustache behaviour.
- Natural facial movement.
- Subtle emotional transitions.

Avoid exaggerated animation or dramatic hero acting.

Father should always feel like the same man expressing emotion naturally.

---

# Continuity Rules

Always maintain:

- Grounded expressions.
- Calm eyes.
- Warm restrained smiles.
- Natural emotion.
- Understated reactions.

Never:

- Overact.
- Use exaggerated cartoon expressions.
- Display extreme emotional swings without strong narrative justification.
- Sacrifice authenticity for visual drama.
- Turn concern into rage or smile into glamorous posing.
